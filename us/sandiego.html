EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

San Diego's housing market in 2026 earns its reputation as America's most balanced major California market — appreciating steadily, rarely crashing, and supported by a uniquely diversified economic base. The Case-Shiller San Diego index (SDXRSA) reached an all-time high of approximately 447 in January 2025, representing a 347% increase since January 2000. The December 2025 county median SF price hit $1,000,000 for the first time. The 2026 consensus projects +2–4% appreciation, with a 99% sale-to-list ratio signaling sustained demand.

San Diego's “three-engine economy” — military (115,000+ active duty), biotech/life sciences (400+ companies, #3 U.S. hub), and technology (semiconductor, wireless, defense tech) — creates a demand floor materially more resilient than single-sector economies. No one sector's downturn eliminates more than 30–35% of the county's high-income buyer pool.

The 2025 mid-cycle revealed San Diego's characteristic pattern: Jan median +11% YoY → Oct softening to $985K (-2.48%) as inventory rose +14% → Dec recovery to $1M (+2.6%) on seasonal tightening. Unlike Austin's bust or Boston's relentless ascent, San Diego mean-reverts to 3–5% annual appreciation. This predictability makes it one of the most attractive long-term investment markets in the continental U.S.

2026年聖地牙哥住宅市場贏得了美國最均衡主要加州市場的聲譽。Case-Shiller聖地牙哥指數（SDXRSA）在2025年1月達到約447的歷史高點，代表自2000年1月以來上漲347%。2025年12月縣獨棟住宅中位數首次達到100萬美元。2026年共識預測為+2至4%升值，99%的成交/報價比顯示持續需求。

聖地牙哥的「三引擎經濟」——軍事（115,000名以上現役）、生技/生命科學（400家以上公司，美國第三大中心）、以及科技（半導體、無線、國防科技）——創造了比單一行業經濟更具韌性的需求底部。沒有任何一個行業的下行會消除超過30至35%的縣高收入買家群體。

2025年中週期揭示了聖地牙哥的典型模式：1月中位數年增+11%→10月庫存年增14%後軟化至$98.5萬（-2.48%）→12月季節性收緊後恢復至$100萬（+2.6%）。與奧斯丁的崩潰或波士頓的無情攀升不同，聖地牙哥均值回歸到每年3至5%的升值路徑。這種可預測性使其成為北美大陸最具吸引力的長期投資市場之一。

PRICE TREND: ALL-TIME HIGH CS & MID-CYCLE CORRECTION房價走勢：CS歷史高點與中週期修正

San Diego's CS index trajectory from 2015 to 2025 is the most consistent of any California market. The SDXRSA rose from approximately 225 in January 2015 to 447 in January 2025 — a 99% increase over the decade, achieved with the smallest maximum drawdown of any Western U.S. CS metro: a 9% peak-to-trough in 2022–2023, fully recovered in 24 months.

The median price history: $475K (2015), $560K (2019), $680K (2020), $825K (2021), $950K (2022 peak), $875K (2023 trough), $975K (2024), $1,000,000 (December 2025). The pandemic phase produced approximately 40% nominal appreciation from January 2020 to mid-2022 peak — significant but well below Austin's 80%. The December 2025 $1M milestone means San Diego has joined the California coastal $1M+ club alongside Los Angeles, San Francisco, and the Bay Area.

聖地牙哥的CS指數走勢是任何加州市場中最一致的。SDXRSA從2015年1月的約225升至2025年1月的447——十年漲幅99%，以西部美國任何CS大都市中最小的最大回撤實現：2022至2023年峰值至谷值9%，24個月完全恢復。

中位數價格歷史：$47.5萬（2015年）、$56萬（2019年）、$68萬（2020年）、$82.5萬（2021年）、$95萬（2022年峰值）、$87.5萬（2023年谷底）、$97.5萬（2024年）、100萬美元（2025年12月）。疫情階段產生從2020年1月至2022年中峰值約40%的名義漲幅。2025年12月$100萬里程碑意味著聖地牙哥已加入加州沿海$100萬以上俱樂部。

THE THREE-ENGINE ECONOMY: MILITARY, BIOTECH & TECH三引擎經濟：軍事、生技與科技

San Diego's economic resilience is best explained by its unique three-engine structure. Military: 115,000+ active duty, 145,000+ dependents, 100,000+ civilian support, defense contractors (Northrop Grumman, BAE Systems, General Atomics, SAIC), defense spending exceeding $30 billion annually. Biotech/Life Sciences: 400+ companies including Illumina, Qualcomm, Thermo Fisher; UCSD and Salk Institute as academic anchors; 70,000+ total employment at $120K+ average compensation. Technology: semiconductor, wireless, defense tech concentrated in Sorrento Valley and Rancho Bernardo corridors.

A recession that devastates tech hiring does not reduce aircraft carrier deployments; a biotech funding drought does not reduce the 32nd Street Naval Base; an AI boom that replaces software engineers does not replace biotech researchers. This non-correlation is the central reason San Diego has appreciated in 9 of the last 10 years.

聖地牙哥的經濟韌性由其獨特的三引擎結構最好地解釋。軍事：115,000名以上現役、145,000名以上家屬、100,000名以上平民支持，國防承包商，國防開支年超300億美元。生技/生命科學：400家以上公司包括Illumina、高通、賽默飛世爾；UCSD和沙克研究所作為學術錨點；70,000以上就業，平均薪酬$12萬以上。科技：半導體、無線、國防科技集中在索倫托谷和蘭喬伯納多走廊。

摧毀科技招聘的衰退不會減少航母部署；生技資金乾旱不會縮減第32街海軍基地；取代軟件工程師的AI繁榮不會取代生技研究員。這種非相關性是聖地牙哥過去10年中9年實現升值的核心原因。

NEIGHBORHOOD DIVERGENCE: COASTAL VS INLAND VS NORTH COUNTY社區分化：沿海 VS 內陸 VS 北縣

San Diego County encompasses 70+ zip codes and approximately 3,500 square miles. Coastal enclaves (La Jolla, Del Mar, Coronado) represent the trophy tier — La Jolla median exceeds $2.5M, 68% cash buyers in the luxury segment, and 8–9% annual appreciation. The tech corridor (Sorrento Valley, Mira Mesa, 4S Ranch) has become San Diego's Sunnyvale: $1.0–1.3M medians with consistent 3–5% annual appreciation from dual-income STEM demand. Coastal North County (Encinitas, Carlsbad) offers the best balance: $1.1–1.4M, top school districts, coastal lifestyle. The inland/suburban tier (Chula Vista, El Cajon, Santee) offers the most affordability ($600K–$800K) and the most rate sensitivity.

聖地牙哥縣包含70多個郵遞區號和約3,500平方英里。沿海飛地（La Jolla、德爾馬、科羅納多）代表精品層——La Jolla中位數超過250萬美元，豪宅板塊68%現金買家，年升值8至9%。科技走廊（索倫托谷、Mira Mesa、4S Ranch）：$100至130萬中位數，STEM雙收入需求驅動年升值3至5%。沿海北縣（恩西尼塔斯、卡爾斯巴德）提供最佳平衡：$110至140萬，頂級學區，沿海生活方式。內陸/郊區層級（丘拉Vista、El Cajon、Santee）：$60至80萬範圍，對利率最敏感。

SUPPLY & INVENTORY: CONSTRAINED BUT NORMALIZING供給與庫存：受限但正常化中

Active listings countywide reached approximately 4,700 in October 2025 — double pandemic lows but well below the 8,000–10,000 typical of pre-2015 balanced markets. The Unsold Inventory Index of 2.5 months (December 2025) remains solidly in seller's market territory. Inventory rose 14% YoY in October without triggering a median price collapse — characteristic of San Diego's balanced demand base.

San Diego is bounded by ocean (west), mountains (east), Mexico (south), and Camp Pendleton (north). California's AB 2011 and SB 9 have loosened some restrictions, but entitlement timelines of 3–7 years limit near-term relief. New SF construction is concentrated in Otay Ranch and similar planned communities on the county's periphery.

縣活躍掛牌在2025年10月達到約4,700套——是疫情低位的兩倍，但仍遠低於均衡的2015年前市場的8,000至10,000套。2025年12月2.5個月的未售庫存指數仍牢固處於賣方市場範圍。庫存在2025年10月年增14%而沒有觸發中位數崩潰——聖地牙哥均衡需求基礎的特徵。

聖地牙哥受海洋（西）、山脈（東）、墨西哥（南）和Camp Pendleton（北）限制。加州AB 2011和SB 9放鬆了部分限制，但3至7年授權時間表限制了近期緩解。新建獨棟住宅集中在Otay Ranch和縣邊緣的類似規劃社區。

RENT CONTROL: AB 1482 & SAN DIEGO LOCAL MEASURES租管：AB 1482與聖地牙哥地方措施

San Diego operates under California's AB 1482 (5%+CPI or 10% cap for pre-2005 non-exempt units) with the City of San Diego's 2023 Tenant Protection Ordinance adding just-cause eviction and enhanced relocation assistance. SFRs, condos, and post-2004 construction have no rent control under Costa-Hawkins. Average rents: coastal La Jolla/Mission Bay $3,500+/month, mid-city $2,650, inland $2,200. Vacancy approximately 4.5–5.0% county-wide — near structural floor as 55% of city residents rent.

聖地牙哥在加州AB 1482框架下運作（2005年前非豁免單位5%+CPI或10%上限），聖地牙哥市的2023年《租戶保護條例》增加了正當原因驅逐和增強搬遷協助。獨棟住宅、公寓和2004年後建設在《科斯塔-霍金斯》下無租管。平均租金：沿海La Jolla/Mission Bay $3,500以上/月，中城$2,650，內陸$2,200。縣空置率約4.5至5.0%——接近結構性底部。

OUTLOOK 2026–2028展望 2026–2028

The base case (65% probability) projects CS San Diego appreciation of 3–5% annually through 2027, driven by continued in-migration from LA (SD is 20% cheaper), the defense spending anchor, and biotech pipeline hiring. The county SF median is projected to reach $1.05–$1.10M by end-2026 and $1.12–$1.18M by end-2027. CS index: 446 → 470–480 by end-2027.

The bull case (20% probability) centers on rate decline to 5.5% plus expanded defense spending driven by Pacific Fleet buildout. This could produce 8–10% appreciation in a 12-month window. CS index could reach 495–510 by end-2027.

The bear case (15%) requires federal defense spending reduction combined with a California-wide insurance crisis. Under this scenario, CS declines to 400–410 (2022–2023 trough levels). This is genuinely low-probability — both risks require simultaneous federal policy change and catastrophic natural event.

基本情境（概率65%）預計CS聖地牙哥每年升值3至5%，縣SF中位數到2026年底達$105至110萬，到2027年底達$112至118萬。CS指數：446→2027年底470至480。

樂觀情境（概率20%）以利率下降至5.5%加上太平洋艦隊建設推動的國防開支擴張為中心。可能在12個月內產生8至10%升值。CS指數到2027年底可能達到495至510。

悲觀情境（概率15%）需要聯邦國防開支削減加上加州全境保險危機。CS下降至400至410（2022至2023年谷底水平）。這是真正的低概率情境。
