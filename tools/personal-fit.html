  • рҹӣӮ Visa вҖ” Difficulty of obtaining a long-term (1yr+) visa. Score is the inverse of the minimum capital requirement across available programs: вүӨ$50K вҶ’ 95, вүӨ$200K вҶ’ 85, вүӨ$500K вҶ’ 75, вүӨ$1M вҶ’ 65, вүӨ$2.5M вҶ’ 50, >$2.5M вҶ’ 35. Visa goal (PR / passport) then adjusts: fast path вҶ’ +10, no path вҶ’ up to -35. 100 if origin = market (no visa needed); 15-25 if all programs closed (e.g. IE Tier 1).
  • рҹ—ЈпёҸ Lang вҖ” Daily-life language friction. Combines market's English usability + Mandarin speaker % + your selected languages. 100 if origin = market (native speaker assumed). EF EPI 2024 source.
  • вңҲпёҸ Flight вҖ” Direct flight hours from your origin. вүӨ2h вҶ’ 98, вүӨ4h вҶ’ 90, вүӨ8h вҶ’ 65, >18h вҶ’ 18. 100 if origin = market.
  • рҹҢЎпёҸ Climate вҖ” Temperature comfort + 5 disaster types averaged (earthquake/typhoon/flood/wildfire/haze). Sweet spot 22-27В°C. Age 50+ penalised for >28В°C. World Bank Climate / UN INDR sources.
  • рҹҸҘ Health вҖ” Numbeo Healthcare Index 2025 + bonus for universal coverage. Age 50+ amplifies high/low scores by Вұ5. WHO + Numbeo sources.
  • рҹҺ“ Edu вҖ” Without kids: weighted by QS top university rank. With kids: international school count + curricula diversity (IB/British/American) + tuition affordability. ISC Research + QS 2025 sources.
