How to read spread: Spread > 3% — property cash flow substantially covers opportunity cost (relatively cheap). Spread 0–3% — thin margin; capital gains required for a positive outcome. Spread < 0% — bonds yield more than rent; property priced for pure appreciation. All yields are gross, pre-tax. Transaction costs, management fees, and vacancy not deducted. 利差解讀： 利差 > 3% — 房產租金現金流大幅覆蓋機會成本（相對便宜）。 利差 0–3% — 利差薄，需靠資本利得才合理。 利差 < 0% — 公債殖利率高於租金，房產定價純靠升值預期。 所有收益率均為毛利率、稅前，未扣稅費、管理費及空置。
