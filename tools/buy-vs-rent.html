Methodology: Buy NPV = PV of all buying costs (mortgage + maintenance 1% + opportunity cost on equity − exit proceeds) discounted at alt return rate. Rent NPV = PV of all rents over horizon. Break-even = year when cumulative buy cost ≤ cumulative rent cost. Defaults are mid-market estimates as of 2026. 方法說明：買入NPV = 買入總成本現值（房貸+維護費1%+股權機會成本－賣出所得），以替代報酬率折現。租屋NPV = 持有期間租金現值。回本年 = 累積買入成本首次低於累積租金的年份。預設值為2026年市場估計值。
