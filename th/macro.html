Monetary Cycle × House Prices貨幣週期 × 房價
What to watch: Thailand's HPI is closely tied to Bangkok condo supply cycles driven by Chinese buyer demand (2016–19) and then the COVID tourism wipeout. BoT held rates flat at 0.50% for three years (2020–22) while other central banks hiked — this shows Thailand's inflation stayed subdued. The 2024 rate cut reflects growth concerns, not overheating.
重點觀察：泰國HPI與曼谷公寓供給週期緊密相關，2016–19年由中國買家需求驅動，隨後遭COVID觀光業重創。BoT在2020–22年其他央行升息期間，利率維持在0.50%不變——這顯示泰國通膨保持溫和。2024年的降息反映的是成長擔憂，而非過熱。

What to watch: Tourism accounts for ~20% of Thai GDP — the 2020 collapse (−6.1% GDP) was the worst shock. The incomplete recovery (35M vs 40M arrivals) explains weak domestic demand. LTV policy tightening in 2019 (3rd property: LTV capped 70%) contributed to 2019–20 condo market correction before COVID hit.
重點觀察：觀光業佔泰國GDP約20%——2020年崩盤（GDP衰退6.1%）是最大衝擊。復甦不完整（3,500萬 vs 4,000萬旅客）解釋了國內需求疲軟。2019年LTV政策收緊（第三套房：LTV上限70%）在COVID來襲前已引發2019–20年公寓市場修正。

Key Cycle Events (2008–2025)關鍵週期事件（2008–2025）
2008–2009 · GFC SHOCK2008–2009 · GFC衝擊
BoT Cuts to 1.25% — Economy ContractsBoT降至1.25%，經濟萎縮
Export collapse and political turmoil (PAD airport seizure, Dec 2008) hit the economy. GDP contracts −2.3% in 2009. BoT cuts from 3.75% to 1.25%. HPI dips mildly. Recovery is quick as global trade rebounds and tourism returns rapidly.出口崩潰加上政治動盪（PAD佔機場事件，2008年12月）衝擊經濟，2009年GDP萎縮2.3%。BoT從3.75%降至1.25%，HPI輕微下跌。隨著全球貿易和觀光業迅速反彈，復甦來得很快。
2010–2014 · PROPERTY BOOM2010–2014 · 房產榮景
Condo Supply Surge — Domestic Demand Drives Market公寓供給激增，國內需求驅動市場
Post-GFC recovery and tourism growth fuel property boom. Bangkok condo launches surge. BoT raises rate to 2.75% (2011) to combat inflation. HPI gains steadily +4–6%/yr. Developer launches concentrate in Bangkok mid-segment (2–5M THB). Rural-urban migration underpins demand.後GFC復甦加觀光業成長帶動房產榮景。曼谷公寓推盤激增，BoT升息至2.75%（2011年）對抗通膨。HPI每年穩定上漲4–6%，開發商推盤集中在曼谷中端市場（200–500萬泰銖），城鄉移民支撐需求。
AUG–OCT 2011 · GREAT FLOOD2011年8–10月 · 世紀大洪災
Flooding Destroys Industrial Estates — GDP Hit −10%洪水摧毀工業區，GDP衝擊達−10%
Worst flooding in 70 years inundates 7 central Thai provinces. Major industrial parks (Ayutthaya, Pathum Thani) submerged — global supply chains disrupted. Ayutthaya and northern Bangkok property values temporarily plunge. Government fast-tracks flood-free zone development in eastern seaboard.70年來最嚴重洪災淹沒泰國中部7個府，大城及巴吞他尼主要工業園區被淹——全球供應鏈中斷。大城和曼谷北部房產價值暫時重挫。政府快速推進東部海岸防洪工業區開發。
2016–2018 · CHINESE BUYER WAVE2016–2018 · 中國買家浪潮
Chinese Investment Fuels Pattaya & Phuket Condos中國投資推動芭提雅和普吉島公寓
Chinese capital outflow restrictions ease post-2015 stock crash. Chinese buyers dominate Pattaya (50%+ of condo sales), Phuket, and central Bangkok. Developers target Chinese market with THB-denominated offshore marketing. HPI accelerates +6–8%. Government imposes developer restrictions on foreign-targeted projects in 2018.2015年股災後中國資本外流管制鬆動，中國買家主導芭提雅（超過50%公寓銷售）、普吉島和曼谷市中心。開發商以泰銖計價的境外行銷瞄準中國市場，HPI加速上漲6–8%。2018年政府對外資主導項目施加限制。
JAN 2019 · LTV TIGHTENING2019年1月 · LTV收緊
BoT Caps 3rd Property LTV at 70% — Condo Sales DropBoT限制第三套房LTV上限70%，公寓銷售下滑
Bank of Thailand implements LTV limits: 2nd property = 90% LTV; 3rd+ = 70% LTV. Target: reduce speculative presale purchases and investment condos. Bangkok condo new launches drop 30% in H1 2019. Inventory overhang builds. Chinese buyer pullback (trade war + capital control tightening) compounds the correction.泰國銀行實施LTV上限：第二套房90%，第三套房以上70%。目標：減少預售投機和投資型公寓。2019年上半年曼谷公寓新推盤量減少30%，庫存積壓累積。中國買家撤退（貿易戰+資本管制收緊）加劇修正。
2020–2021 · COVID — TOURISM COLLAPSES2020–2021 · COVID觀光崩盤
39.8M Tourists → 6.7M — GDP −6.1%旅客從3,980萬→670萬，GDP衰退6.1%
Thailand's borders close March 2020. Tourism collapses −83% in arrivals. GDP −6.1% — worst since 1997 AFC. BoT cuts to historic low 0.50% (May 2020). HPI flat to mild negative. Phuket, Koh Samui, Pattaya property market virtually frozen. Bangkok mid-range condos see quiet secondary market.泰國2020年3月關閉邊境，旅客入境量崩跌83%，GDP衰退6.1%——為1997年亞洲金融危機以來最差。BoT降至歷史低點0.50%（2020年5月）。HPI持平至輕微負成長。普吉島、蘇梅島、芭提雅房市幾乎凍結，曼谷中端公寓二手市場也趨於平靜。
2022–2023 · TOURISM REOPENING2022–2023 · 觀光重開邊境
11.1M (2022) → 28M (2023) — Resort Markets Revive旅客1,110萬（2022）→2,800萬（2023），度假市場復甦
Thailand fully reopens Nov 2022. Tourist arrivals recover to 28M in 2023 — still 70% of pre-COVID peak. BoT hikes to 2.50% (2023) — first hike since 2018. HPI recovers slowly. Chinese tourists remain weak (8M vs 11M in 2019) as China economic slowdown damps outbound. EEC (Eastern Economic Corridor) drives Chonburi/Rayong demand.泰國於2022年11月全面重開邊境，旅客2023年回升至2,800萬——仍僅達COVID前高峰的70%。BoT升息至2.50%（2023年），為2018年以來首次升息。HPI緩慢回升。中國旅客依然疲弱（800萬 vs 2019年的1,100萬），因中國經濟放緩壓抑出境旅遊。東部經濟走廊（EEC）帶動春武里/羅勇需求。
FEB 2025 · BoT SURPRISE CUT2025年2月 · BoT意外降息
Rate Cut to 2.00% on Growth Concerns因應成長憂慮降息至2.00%
BoT cuts 25bp to 2.00% citing weak domestic demand, subdued inflation (0.8%), and tourism recovery still below target. LTV measures partially relaxed for 2024–25 to support market. Bangkok luxury segment gains traction from HNW Southeast Asians. Chinese developers (e.g., Country Garden JVs) pause projects on parent distress. US tariff threat adds export headwind.BoT降息25bp至2.00%，原因是國內需求疲弱、通膨溫和（0.8%），觀光復甦仍低於目標。2024–25年部分放寬LTV措施以支撐市場。曼谷豪宅區受東南亞高淨值人士追捧而有所提振。中國開發商（如碧桂園合資項目）因母公司困境而暫停項目。美國關稅威脅帶來出口逆風。
