Key Risk Factors主要風險因素

Household Debt家庭負債

At 90.6% of GDP, Thailand's household debt constrains domestic demand significantly. The 70% mortgage rejection rate for properties under THB 3 million highlights the affordability crisis in the affordable segment, limiting market breadth and recovery potential.家庭負債佔GDP 90.6%，嚴重制約國內需求。300萬泰銖以下房產70%的拒貸率凸顯平價住宅的負擔能力危機，限制了市場廣度與復甦潛力。

Oversupply供給過剩

Bangkok has 235,000 unsold condo units at record levels. Developers are shifting to low-rise products, but absorption continues to lag new launches. The oversupply is concentrated in mid-range condos along new transit lines where speculative buying was heaviest.曼谷有23.5萬戶未售公寓，創歷史新高。開發商正轉向低層住宅，但去化速度仍落後新推案。供給過剩集中在新捷運沿線的中價位公寓，該區域投機購買最為密集。

Foreign Dependency外資依賴

The market is increasingly reliant on foreign buyers, with Chinese nationals accounting for 48% of foreign condo transfers. Geopolitical shifts or regulatory changes in China could significantly impact demand. The 49% foreign ownership cap per building adds structural constraints.市場日益依賴外國買家，中國籍買家佔外國人公寓過戶的48%。中國的地緣政治變化或監管政策調整可能顯著影響需求。每棟建築49%的外國人持有上限增加了結構性限制。

Currency Risk匯率風險

Thai baht volatility affects foreign investor returns. Strong USD periods reduce the purchasing power of Chinese and Russian buyers, who represent the largest foreign buyer cohorts. Currency depreciation can trigger capital outflows from property holdings.泰銖波動影響外國投資者回報。美元走強時期削弱中國及俄羅斯買家的購買力，而他們是最大的外國買家群體。貨幣貶值可能引發房產持有的資金外流。
