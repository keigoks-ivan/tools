Valuation Framework估值框架

Price-to-Income房價所得比

Bangkok PTI of 8.5x is moderate compared to regional peers such as Taipei (15.4x) and Hong Kong (18x+). However, the domestic segment is under pressure from high household debt at 90.6% of GDP, constraining purchasing power for lower-income buyers.曼谷房價所得比 8.5 倍相較台北（15.4倍）和香港（18倍以上）等區域同儕屬中等水平。然而，家庭負債高達 GDP 的 90.6%，對國內低收入買家的購買力形成壓力。

Price-to-Rent房價租金比

P/R ratio of 31x for Bangkok CBD translates to gross yields of 5%+, still providing positive carry versus the BoT rate of 2.25%. Unlike Taiwan and Hong Kong where yields barely cover borrowing costs, Thailand remains attractive on a rental income basis.曼谷 CBD 租金比 31 倍對應毛收益率 5% 以上，仍可提供相對於央行利率 2.25% 的正利差。不同於台灣和香港的租金收益幾乎無法覆蓋借貸成本，泰國在租金收入層面仍具吸引力。

Yield Spread利差分析

The +2.75% spread is attractive versus near-zero spreads in Taiwan (+0.11%) and negative spreads in Hong Kong, making Thailand a yield play for foreign investors. With the BoT rate at 2.25% after the October 2024 cut, the carry trade remains viable.+2.75% 的利差相較台灣（+0.11%）的近零利差和香港的負利差極具吸引力，使泰國成為外國投資者的收益型投資標的。在 2024 年 10 月降息後央行利率為 2.25%，套利交易仍然可行。
