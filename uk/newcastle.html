SUMMARY總結

Best Value最佳價值

With a price-to-income ratio of 4.5-5.6x, Newcastle is the most affordable major UK city. Average prices of £208K offer entry points far below London (£547K) and Edinburgh (£340K), making it ideal for yield-focused investors.房價所得比4.5-5.6倍，紐卡斯爾是英國最可負擔的主要城市。均價£208K遠低於倫敦（£547K）和愛丁堡（£340K），是追求報酬率投資者的理想選擇。

Rental Growth Leader租金成長領先

Monthly rents surging +15.5% YoY to £1,190 reflect acute undersupply. With ~50,000 students and growing professional demand, Newcastle's rental market shows no signs of cooling.月租金年增+15.5%至£1,190反映嚴重供不應求。約50,000名學生和日益增長的專業人士需求，紐卡斯爾租賃市場毫無降溫跡象。
