SUMMARY總結

Student Market學生市場

With four major universities and strong international enrolment, Edinburgh's student rental market provides a reliable demand floor. Purpose-built student accommodation (PBSA) remains undersupplied.擁有四所主要大學和強勁的國際招生，愛丁堡的學生租賃市場提供可靠的需求支撐。專建學生住宿（PBSA）仍然供不應求。

Heritage Premium遺產溢價

UNESCO World Heritage status and Edinburgh Festival tourism create a unique price premium in the Old and New Town. Short-let regulation tightening may redirect capital toward long-term rental strategies.UNESCO世界遺產地位和愛丁堡藝術節旅遊在舊城和新城創造獨特的價格溢價。短租法規收緊可能將資本導向長期租賃策略。
