Renters Rights Act租客權益法案

HIGH高

Section 21 no-fault evictions abolished from May 2026. Landlords face reduced flexibility, potentially accelerating BTL portfolio exits and tightening rental supply further.2026年5月起廢除第21條無過失驅逐。房東靈活性降低，可能加速買房出租組合退出，進一步收緊租賃供給。

Stamp Duty Headwind印花稅逆風

HIGH高

Temporary stamp duty relief ended April 2025. Higher transaction costs now dampen demand, particularly for first-time buyers at the margin and investors facing the 3% surcharge.臨時印花稅寬減於2025年4月結束。較高的交易成本抑制需求，尤其是邊際首次購房者及面臨3%附加費的投資者。

EPC Regulation能源效率法規

MEDIUM中

Tightening EPC requirements from 2026 force landlords to invest in energy upgrades. Properties below minimum standards face lettings ban, creating capex pressure and potential stock reduction.2026年起收緊的能源效率要求迫使房東投資能源升級。低於最低標準的房產面臨出租禁令，產生資本支出壓力及潛在存量減少。

Macro Slowdown總經放緩

MEDIUM中

UK GDP growth at +1.4% remains below potential. Persistent cost-of-living pressures and global trade uncertainty weigh on consumer confidence and housing demand recovery.英國GDP成長率+1.4%仍低於潛在水平。持續的生活成本壓力與全球貿易不確定性拖累消費者信心與住房需求復甦。
