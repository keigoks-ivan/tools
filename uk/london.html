SUMMARY總結

Yield Warning收益率警示

London yields of 3–4% sit below the BoE base rate of 3.75%, resulting in negative carry for leveraged investors. Cash buyers only territory in prime zones.倫敦3–4%的收益率低於英格蘭銀行基準利率3.75%，對槓桿投資者造成負利差。黃金地段僅適合現金買家。

Foreign Capital外資

Approximately 25% of prime central London transactions involve overseas buyers. This capital flow supports floor prices but increases exposure to geopolitical and regulatory risk.約25%黃金地段交易涉及海外買家。此資金流支撐價格底部，但增加地緣政治與監管風險敞口。
