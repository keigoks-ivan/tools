SUMMARY總結

Investment Hotspot投資熱點

Manchester's 6.5% average yield with a +2.75% spread over the BoE base rate delivers strong positive carry. Hotspot areas reach up to 12%, making it one of the UK's top rental markets.曼徹斯特平均6.5%報酬率，較英格蘭銀行基準利率高出+2.75%，帶來強勁正向利差。熱點區域高達12%，為英國頂級租賃市場之一。

Growth Driver成長動力

New build prices have surged +26% over five years (2020–2025), driven by the Northern Powerhouse initiative, Media City expansion, and strong migration from London-priced-out professionals.新屋價格五年內飆漲+26%（2020–2025），受北部引擎計畫、媒體城擴張及從倫敦外溢的專業人士推動。
