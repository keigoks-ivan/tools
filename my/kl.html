KL's luxury segment is caught between two forces. Transactions above RM1M grew 6.5% in 2024, and top-decile rentals reached RM5,295/month. Yet overhang remains concentrated in RM1M+ and SOHO categories, meaning pockets of oversupply persist even as headline demand improves.

KLCC and Bukit Bintang attract foreign buyers at RM1,200–2,500+ PSF, with 8–12% discounts available from distressed or motivated sellers. In contrast, Bangsar and Mont Kiara in the RM1.2–1.6M range show stronger fundamentals with 4.5–6% gross yields, supported by established expatriate demand and limited new supply.

Branded residences have proven most resilient — TRX Residences from RM1.2M, Pavilion Bukit Bintang from RM1.7M, and Alila 2 Bangsar at ~RM1,200 PSF. For qualified buyers, the current window is the most favourable in 5 years, combining price correction, rental growth, and selective discounts.

吉隆坡豪宅市場正處於兩股力量的拉扯之中。2024年RM1M以上交易量增長6.5%，前十分位租金達RM5,295/月。然而積壓仍集中在RM1M+及SOHO類別，意味著即使整體需求改善，局部過剩依然存在。

KLCC及武吉免登以RM1,200–2,500+呎價吸引外國買家，急售或有動機的賣家提供8–12%的折扣。相比之下，滿家樂及滿都卡拉（Mont Kiara）RM1.2–1.6M價位展現更強勁的基本面，毛收益率4.5–6%，得益於穩定的外籍人士需求和有限的新供應。

品牌住宅表現最具韌性——TRX Residences起價RM1.2M、Pavilion武吉免登起價RM1.7M、Alila 2 Bangsar約RM1,200呎價。對於合格買家而言，當前視窗是過去5年最有利的，結合了價格修正、租金增長及選擇性折扣。

The key shift: RM1M+ national overhang collapsed 41% from 4,173 units (2023) to 2,463 units (2024), while sub-RM500K overhang expanded. High-end absorption is improving. But KL completions surged 32.7% YoY in Q1–Q3 2025 (7,418 units) while starts fell 14.9% — meaning the delivery wave peaks in 2026 before thinning.
關鍵轉折：全國RM1M+待售餘屋從2023年的4,173套大幅萎縮41%至2024年的2,463套，而RM50萬以下的積壓反而持續擴大。高端段的吸納能力正在改善。但KL 2025年前三季完工戶數年增32.7%（7,418套），同時開工量卻年減14.9%——意味著交屋潮在2026年達到頂峰後將快速消退。

Yield vs capital gain is an inverse relationship in KL luxury: Projects with highest gross yields (7%+) show flat or declining capital values. Ultra-luxury with 2% yields show 30-59% capital appreciation. Net yield after costs and 30% non-resident tax: ~1.1–2.1% — competitive vs Singapore (sub-2%) and Hong Kong (sub-1.5%) but not passive income.
KL豪宅市場：收益率與資本增值是反向關係。毛收益率最高的項目（7%+）往往資本價值停滯甚至下跌；超豪華物業收益率僅2%，卻錄得30-59%的資本增值。扣除持有成本和30%非居民稅後，淨收益率約1.1-2.1%——雖優於新加坡（不到2%）和香港（不到1.5%），但稱不上被動收入。

Three catalysts define the next decade: MRT3 Circle Line (51.6km, 33 stations, 2032 completion) will unlock Mont Kiara's transit premium. Against these, 2026 stamp duty to 8% and MYR appreciation represent headwinds for foreign capital.
三大催化劑定義未來十年：MRT3環線（51.6公里、33站、2032年通車）將解鎖蒙吉拉的交通溢價。TRX成熟化（45,000名常駐上班族、Exchange TRX購物中心全面運營、匯豐和保誠進駐）將收斂新區折價。馬來西亞城（486英畝、GDV1,400億令吉、2026年動工）在50年周期內重塑南KL格局。但2026年印花稅升至8%和馬幣升值，構成外資進場的阻力。
