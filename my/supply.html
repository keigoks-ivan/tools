Base case (absorption 85,000/yr): overhang peaks at ~47,000 units in 2027, normalising to ~14,000 by 2029. Bull case (90,000/yr absorption, OPR stays low): peak ~42,000, clears faster to ~8,000. 1-in-3 threshold = systemic concern · Source: NAPIC每3戶完工1戶賣不出=系統性警戒 · 來源：NAPIC

Why this metric matters more than absolute units: Malaysia has 6.3 million existing residential units — expressing overhang as a share of total stock yields a misleadingly small number (~0.5%). The more meaningful lens is what fraction of newly-completed homes remain unsold. At the 2019 peak, nearly 1 in 2 completed units went unsold — a level that signals genuine developer overreach, not just normal inventory churn. The 2025Q3 reading of 41% is back near peak territory, driven by a surge in completions rather than a collapse in demand. Watch this ratio, not the absolute number. 為何這個指標比絕對數量更重要：馬來西亞有630萬戶住宅存量——用餘屋佔總存量的比例衡量，會得出一個具誤導性的小數字（約0.5%）。更有意義的視角是每年新完工的房屋中有多少比例賣不出去。在2019年高峰期，接近每2戶完工就有1戶滯銷——這個水準顯示開發商供給嚴重過剩，而非正常的庫存流動。2025年Q3的41%再度接近峰值，主因是完工量激增，而非需求崩潰。追蹤這個比率，而非絕對數字。
