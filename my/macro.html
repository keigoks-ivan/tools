What to watch: When OPR cuts follow M2 expansion, MHPI typically lags by 12–18 months. The 2020–21 OPR floor (1.75%) combined with M2 surge pre-loaded the price recovery seen in 2022–24.
重點觀察：當OPR降息伴隨M2擴張，MHPI通常滯後12–18個月跟漲。2020–21年OPR低點（1.75%）加上M2激增，預先累積了2022–24年的房價回升動力。

2008–2009 · GFC SHOCK2008–2009 · GFC衝擊
BNM Cuts OPR to 2.00%BNM將OPR降至2.00%
Three cuts from 3.50% as global trade collapses. GDP contracts −1.6% in 2009 — worst since Asian Financial Crisis. MHPI dips −1.5% but recovers quickly.全球貿易崩潰，BNM三度降息。2009年GDP衰退1.6%，為亞洲金融危機以來最差。MHPI微跌1.5%後迅速反彈。
2010–2013 · PROPERTY BOOM2010–2013 · 房產榮景
ETP Drives M2 Surge to 14.6%ETP計劃推動M2達14.6%高峰
Economic Transformation Programme unleashes infrastructure spend. Credit floods property sector. MHPI gains +34% in four years. Housing loan growth peaks at 14% (2010).經濟轉型計劃釋放基建支出，信貸湧入房產業。MHPI四年累漲34%，房貸成長率於2010年達14%高峰。
JUL 2014 · TIGHTENING BEGINS2014年7月 · 收緊開始
BNM Hikes to 3.25% + TDSR/LTV CapsBNM升息至3.25%，收緊TDSR/LTV上限
Government introduces Total Debt Service Ratio limits and tighter LTV rules. Speculative demand curbed. Property overhang starts building. MHPI growth decelerates sharply.政府實施TDSR限制及更嚴格LTV規定，投機需求受抑，房產積壓開始累積，MHPI升速急劇放緩。
APR 2015 · GST SHOCK2015年4月 · GST衝擊
6% GST Raises Construction Costs6% GST推高建設成本
Goods & Services Tax raises input costs across the construction supply chain. Developers pass through costs; affordability deteriorates. OPR cut to 3.00% in July 2016 provides partial relief.消費稅推高建築供應鏈成本，發展商轉嫁給買家，可負擔性惡化。2016年7月OPR降至3.00%提供部分紓緩。
2020 · COVID SHOCK2020年 · COVID衝擊
4 Cuts → OPR Historic Low 1.75%4次降息 → OPR創歷史低點1.75%
Economy contracts −5.5% — worst in Malaysian history. BNM fires four consecutive cuts. Government launches PENJANA and PEMULIH stimulus. Loan moratorium deployed. MHPI near-flat (+0.4%).GDP衰退5.5%，創馬來西亞歷史最差。BNM連降四次息，政府推出PENJANA及PEMULIH刺激方案，貸款延期還款。MHPI近乎持平。
2021 · STIMULUS PEAK2021年 · 刺激高峰
HOC Drives Volume, Loans Held at 1.75%HOC推動交易量，OPR維持1.75%
Home Ownership Campaign (HOC) exemptions boost transaction volumes. GDP rebounds +3.3%. Loan growth stays at 6.5%. MHPI barely moves (+0.2%) — demand absorbed overhang stock.HOC豁免措施刺激交易量，GDP反彈3.3%，房貸成長維持6.5%。MHPI幾乎不動（+0.2%）——需求消化積壓庫存。
2022–2023 · RATE HIKE CYCLE2022–2023 · 升息週期
5 Hikes: 1.75% → 3.00%5次升息：1.75% → 3.00%
BNM normalises policy against global inflation. Despite rising rates, MHPI accelerates +7.5% (2022) and +5.6% (2023) — pent-up demand and ringgit weakness drive foreign buyer interest.BNM因應全球通膨正常化利率。儘管升息，MHPI於2022年加速上漲7.5%，2023年漲5.6%——積壓需求及令吉弱勢吸引外國買家。
