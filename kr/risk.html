Key Risk Factors主要風險因素

Household Debt家庭負債

105% of GDP, highest in Asia. DSR cap limits new borrowing but existing debt stock creates systemic vulnerability. Any sharp rate increase would put severe pressure on overleveraged households.佔GDP 105%，為亞洲最高。DSR上限限制新增借貸，但現有債務存量構成系統性風險。任何急劇升息都將對過度槓桿的家庭造成嚴重壓力。

Demographics人口結構

Fertility rate 0.72 (world's lowest), population declining since 2020. Seoul metro aging rapidly, long-term demand destruction for non-prime areas. Without immigration reform, structural demand erosion is inevitable.生育率 0.72（全球最低），人口自 2020 年起持續下降。首爾都會區快速老齡化，非核心區域面臨長期需求萎縮。若無移民政策改革，結構性需求侵蝕不可避免。

Jeonse System全租房制度

Over 1,000 trillion won in outstanding jeonse deposits. System unwinding creates both fraud risk and market dislocation. Insurance coverage still incomplete, leaving tenants exposed to landlord default.全租保證金餘額超過 1,000 兆韓元。制度瓦解同時帶來詐騙風險和市場失序。保險覆蓋仍不完整，租戶面臨房東違約風險。

Policy Whiplash政策反覆

Korea oscillates between extreme tightening and easing based on political cycle, creates investment uncertainty. Regulation changes can be retroactive, adding risk for long-term capital deployment.韓國依據政治週期在極端緊縮與寬鬆之間擺盪，造成投資不確定性。監管變化可具追溯效力，增加長期資本配置的風險。
