Valuation Framework估值框架

Price-to-Income房價所得比

Seoul PTI 15x among world's worst, requires 15+ years household income. Gangnam at 22x virtually inaccessible for ordinary households, rivaling Hong Kong's unaffordability levels.首爾房價所得比 15 倍為全球最高之一，需 15 年以上家庭收入。江南區高達 22 倍，對普通家庭幾乎不可企及，堪比香港的不可負擔水平。

Price-to-Rent房價租金比

P/R 40x implies 2.5% gross yield, deeply below BOK 3.0%. Negative carry for leveraged investors makes Seoul a pure capital appreciation bet, with rental income failing to cover borrowing costs.租金比 40 倍意味著 2.5% 的毛收益率，遠低於韓國央行 3.0% 的利率。槓桿投資者面臨負利差，首爾純屬資本增值博弈，租金收入無法覆蓋借貸成本。

Jeonse Factor全租房因素

Unique jeonse system distorts traditional valuation metrics. Declining jeonse ratio may gradually normalize P/R closer to regional peers. As the system unwinds, rental yields could improve but at the cost of price support erosion.獨特的全租房制度扭曲了傳統估值指標。全租比率下降可能使租金比逐漸正常化至接近區域同儕水平。隨著制度轉變，租金收益率可能改善，但代價是價格支撐的削弱。
