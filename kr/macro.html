Monetary Cycle × House Prices貨幣週期 × 房價
What to watch: Korea's unique Jeonse system (lump-sum rental deposit instead of monthly rent) creates a leverage multiplier in the housing market. When Jeonse prices fall, landlords who used deposits as collateral face a squeeze — triggering the 2022–23 "Jeonse fraud" crisis affecting 150,000+ households. KB national HPI direction is driven by Seoul Gangnam and Mapo/Yongsan districts, which structurally outperform the national index.
重點觀察：韓國獨特的全租金（Jeonse）制度（一次性押金取代月租）在房市中形成槓桿放大效應。當全租金下跌時，以押金作抵押的房東面臨資金緊縮——引發2022–23年「全租金詐騙」危機，影響超過15萬戶家庭。KB全國HPI走勢主要由首爾江南及麻浦/龍山地區帶動，長期跑贏全國指數。

Real Economy × House Prices實體經濟 × 房價
What to watch: Korea's household debt at ~100% of GDP is the highest in the OECD — a structural vulnerability. The 2023 PF (Project Financing) loan crisis, where regional developers and construction firms used short-term PF loans to finance unsold housing, put stress on savings banks and mutual savings institutions. BoK cannot cut aggressively because of household debt sensitivity to rate changes.
重點觀察：韓國家庭債務約佔GDP 100%，為OECD最高——是結構性弱點。2023年PF（項目融資）貸款危機中，地方開發商和建築企業以短期PF貸款融資未售出住宅，對儲蓄銀行和互助儲蓄機構造成壓力。由於家庭對利率變動高度敏感，BoK無法大幅降息。

2008–2009 · GFC SHOCK2008–2009 · GFC衝擊
BoK Cuts to 2.00% — KB HPI StagnatesBoK降至2.00%，KB住宅指數停滯
GFC triggers rapid BoK cuts from 5.25% to 2.00% in six months. Korea's diversified export base limits GDP damage (−0.7% in 2009 — shallower than peers). KB national HPI dips modestly. Government launches large fiscal stimulus (FY2009 budget +5% of GDP). Recovery is swift: GDP rebounds +6.8% in 2010.GFC觸發BoK在六個月內從5.25%快速降至2.00%。韓國多元化出口基礎限制了GDP損失（2009年−0.7%，淺於同儕）。KB全國住宅指數小幅下滑。政府推出大規模財政刺激（2009年財政年度預算增加相當於GDP的5%）。復甦迅速，2010年GDP反彈+6.8%。
2011–2019 · SLOW GRIND HIGHER2011–2019 · 緩慢上行
BoK Cuts Repeatedly to 1.25% — Seoul OutperformsBoK多次降息至1.25%，首爾跑贏全國
BoK cuts rates nine times 2012–2016, reaching historic low of 1.25%. National HPI rises gradually +2–4%/yr, but Seoul districts (Gangnam, Mapo) surge +20–30%. Jeonse prices rise faster than purchase prices — a warning sign of leveraged demand. Government introduces LTV (70%) and DTI (50%) caps from 2017. Chonsei fraud begins emerging.BoK在2012–2016年九次降息至歷史低點1.25%。全國住宅指數每年緩慢上漲2–4%，但首爾各區（江南、麻浦）暴漲20–30%。全租金漲速超過購房價格，是槓桿需求的警示信號。政府從2017年起引入LTV（70%）和DTI（50%）上限。全租金詐騙案開始浮現。
2020–2021 · COVID SURGE2020–2021 · COVID暴漲
BoK to 0.50% + K-New Deal Stimulus — HPI +20%BoK降至0.50%+韓國新政刺激，HPI+20%
BoK hits historic low 0.50% (May 2020). Government's K-New Deal (KRW 160tn) + emergency transfers. Seoul apartment prices surge +25% (2020–21). Young Koreans panic-buy ("영끌" — leveraging everything). Jeonse prices spike as buyers convert to purchase, tightening rental supply. Government imposes aggressive lending caps: LTV/DSR tightened multiple rounds.BoK降至歷史低點0.50%（2020年5月）。政府160兆韓元新政+緊急補貼。首爾公寓價格2020–21年暴漲25%。年輕韓國人恐慌性搶購（「영끌」——傾盡一切加槓桿）。隨著買家轉為購房，全租金急漲，租賃供給收緊。政府多輪收緊LTV/DSR貸款上限。
APR 2022 – JAN 2023 · RATE SHOCK2022年4月–2023年1月 · 升息衝擊
7 Hikes: 0.50% → 3.50% — HPI Falls −10%7次升息：0.50%→3.50%，HPI下跌−10%
BoK raises rates seven times to 3.50% — largest tightening since 1997 AFC. KB national HPI falls −9.5% peak-to-trough. Seoul Gangnam down −12%. Jeonse prices crash −20–30%: landlords who borrowed against rising Jeonse are trapped. Nationwide Jeonse fraud scandal emerges — 150K+ victim cases, KRW 3tn+ in losses.BoK七次升息至3.50%——為1997年亞洲金融危機以來最大緊縮。KB全國住宅指數從峰值下跌9.5%，首爾江南跌幅達12%。全租金價格暴跌20–30%：以上漲全租金作抵押借貸的房東陷入困境。全國全租金詐騙醜聞爆發——超過15萬受害者，損失逾3兆韓元。
2023 · PROJECT FINANCING CRISIS2023年 · 項目融資危機
PF Loans at Risk — Savings Banks Stress-TestedPF貸款面臨違約，儲蓄銀行壓力測試
Korea's Project Financing system — where developers borrow 70–80% LTV from savings banks using unsold units as collateral — faces defaults as construction costs rise and sales slow. FSC estimates KRW 230tn in PF exposure. Government launches KRW 50tn real estate PF support program. Several small savings banks face liquidity stress. Legoland Korea default triggers market shock (Oct 2022).韓國項目融資制度——開發商以未售出單位作抵押，向儲蓄銀行借入LTV高達70–80%——因建設成本上升和銷售放緩而面臨違約。FSC估計PF敞口達230兆韓元。政府推出50兆韓元房地產PF支援計劃，多家小型儲蓄銀行面臨流動性壓力。樂天世界（樂高樂園）韓國違約引發市場衝擊（2022年10月）。
2024 · MARKET BIFURCATION2024年 · 市場嚴重分化
Seoul Rebounds; Provincial Markets Still Deep in Correction首爾反彈；地方市場仍深度修正中
BoK begins cutting: 3.50% → 3.00% (Oct 2024) → 2.75% (Feb 2025). Seoul Gangnam and Mapo see renewed demand — HPI recovering +3–5% in H2 2024. But Incheon, Daejeon, regional cities: unsold inventory reaches decade highs, prices still falling. Government introduces "Non-Gangnam Revitalisation" special zones. Youth homeownership loans (금융 디딤돌) expanded.BoK開始降息：3.50%→3.00%（2024年10月）→2.75%（2025年2月）。首爾江南和麻浦需求重現，2024年下半年HPI回升3–5%。但仁川、大田及地方城市未售庫存達十年高點，房價仍在下跌。政府推出「非江南振興」特區，擴大青年購房貸款（金融基石）。

President Yoon Suk-yeol declares martial law (Dec 3, 2024), revoked hours later after National Assembly vote. Yoon impeached Dec 14. KRW weakens −5% vs USD, KOSPI −3%. Property market sentiment temporarily chilled. BoK keeps rates stable amid political uncertainty. Acting government maintains fiscal stance; housing policy continuity preserved.尹錫悅總統宣布戒嚴（2024年12月3日），數小時後因國會投票而撤銷。尹錫悅於12月14日遭到彈劾。韓元對美元貶值5%，綜合股指跌3%。房市情緒暫時受壓。BoK在政治不確定性下維持利率穩定，代理政府維持財政立場，住房政策延續。
