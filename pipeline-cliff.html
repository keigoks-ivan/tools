⚠ Methodology & classification caveats方法與分類注意事項
Quarterly delivery profiles built from official supply reports (CMHC, Colliers, C&W, NAPIC, Cotality, REIC, BSP) plus developer announcements. Slippage is the rule — Dubai's 2026 record showed only 48% of forecast units actually completing on schedule. We modeled with that slippage where the source provided it; otherwise straight-line allocation. Use as directional signal, not a precise spreadsheet.

Classification matters. Several markets carry parallel inventory categories that headline "residential" figures EXCLUDE:
  • 🇲🇾 KL — serviced apartments (commercial title): 17,883 unsold nationally vs 3,643 residential overhang in KL alone. Most concentrated in Bukit Bintang / Cheras / KL fringe.
  • 🇻🇳 HCMC — officetels & condotels (commercial / tourism title): not in residential pipeline figures.
  • 🇹🇭 Bangkok — 49% foreign quota stranding: ~68% of 220K unsold are in outer suburbs OR locked under Thai-only quota slots that international buyers cannot purchase. Headline overhang overstates the addressable supply for foreigners.
  • 🇸🇬 Singapore — public HDB (~80% of stock) excluded; only private residential pipeline shown.
  • 🇭🇰 Hong Kong — public housing (HOS, public rental) tracked separately from private market.
Always read the SOURCE category before drawing conclusions about a city's true supply pressure. 每季交屋曲線由各國官方供給報告（CMHC、Colliers、C&W、NAPIC、Cotality、REIC、BSP）＋開發商公告建立。延遲是常態 — 杜拜 2026 紀錄顯示僅 48% 預定交屋準時完成。來源提供時採用實際延遲率，否則線性分布。視為方向訊號，非精確試算表。

分類影響真實過剩量。多數市場有「住宅」標題數字未涵蓋的平行庫存類別：
  • 🇲🇾 吉隆坡 — 服務式公寓（商業地契）：全馬 17,883 戶未售，KL 住宅僅 3,643 戶。主要集中武吉免登／蕉賴／市區邊緣。
  • 🇻🇳 胡志明 — Officetel 與 condotel（商業／旅遊地契）：不在住宅在建統計內。
  • 🇹🇭 曼谷 — 49% 外國配額擱淺：22 萬戶未售中約 68% 位於外圍郊區或鎖在「泰人專屬」配額位、外國人買不到。標題過剩量高估了外國人實際可進場的供給。
  • 🇸🇬 新加坡 — HDB 公屋（佔 80% 存量）排除在外；僅顯示私宅在建。
  • 🇭🇰 香港 — 公屋（HOS、公屋）與私宅分開統計。
