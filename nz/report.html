EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

New Zealand's housing market is at a fragile inflection in May 2026. The RBNZ cut the OCR from 5.50% to 2.25% (a 325 basis-point easing cycle) between August 2024 and early 2026, then held at 2.25% on 8 April 2026. The next OCR decision lands on 27 May 2026 — and ANZ, ASB and Infometrics now expect three OCR hikes this year (potentially taking the rate back to 3.00%), driven by Middle East energy-price spillover into headline inflation. Banks have already moved: ANZ's two-year fixed rose to 5.29% and ASB to 5.25%, well above the late-2025 lows. According to REINZ March 2026 data, Auckland's HPI is down -1.21% YoY, while regional markets surprise: Canterbury sales +23.6%, Marlborough +18.9%, West Coast +18.6%, Southland +18.2%. The rental market is softening — national median rent fell -1.6% to NZ$610/week, and rental listing stock surged +21.2%, compressing investor yields just as borrowing costs are rising again.

紐西蘭住宅市場2026年5月處於脆弱的轉折點。RBNZ自2024年8月至2026年初將OCR從5.50%降至2.25%（累計降息325個基點），並於2026年4月8日將利率維持於2.25%。下次OCR決議落在2026年5月27日——ANZ、ASB 與 Infometrics 現在預期 2026 年將升息三次（可能把利率推回3.00%），主因中東能源價格傳導至通膨。銀行已先行動作：ANZ 兩年期固定房貸升至5.29%、ASB 升至5.25%，遠高於 2025 年末低點。REINZ 2026 年 3 月資料顯示：奧克蘭 HPI 年減-1.21%，但區域市場令人意外——坎特伯雷成交+23.6%、馬爾堡+18.9%、西海岸+18.6%、南地+18.2%。租賃市場走軟——全國週租中位數下降-1.6%至NZ$610，出租房源激增+21.2%，借款成本回升之際，投資人收益率受擠壓。

OCR AT 2.25% — HIKE CYCLE NOW PRICED INOCR 2.25%——市場已 PRICE IN 升息週期

The RBNZ delivered 325 basis points of cumulative cuts (from 5.50% to 2.25%) between August 2024 and the start of 2026, then held the OCR at 2.25% on 8 April 2026 — calling explicit attention to Middle East inflation upside risks. Mortgage rates have stopped following the OCR down: the two-year fixed troughed in late 2025 and is now back at 5.25–5.29% (ASB / ANZ) as wholesale curves price in hikes. For a NZ$700,000 loan, monthly repayments that bottomed near $3,650 have climbed back to roughly $4,200. ANZ, ASB and Infometrics now publicly forecast three OCR hikes in 2026, most likely starting in July, taking the OCR back to 3.00%. A May 27 hike is not ruled out. The cycle has flipped from easing tailwind to tightening headwind in just two quarters.

RBNZ自2024年8月至2026年初累計降息325個基點（從5.50%降至2.25%），並於2026年4月8日將OCR維持於2.25%——明確指出中東地緣引發的通膨上行風險。房貸利率已停止跟隨OCR下行：兩年期固定房貸於2025年末觸底，目前回升至5.25–5.29%（ASB / ANZ），因批發市場已 price in 升息。以NZ$70萬貸款為例，月供從低點$3,650反彈至約$4,200。ANZ、ASB 與 Infometrics 現公開預估 2026 年將升息三次，最可能 7 月起步，把 OCR 推回3.00%。5 月 27 日升息也非完全排除。短短兩季，週期從寬鬆順風翻轉為緊縮逆風。

SUPPLY: LISTINGS UP, RENTAL GLUT供給：掛牌增加，租賃過剩

New listings rose +5.5% year-on-year nationally, adding to an already elevated stock of homes for sale. Auckland's inventory has ballooned to 31 weeks of supply — firmly in buyer's market territory. The rental market is experiencing a structural oversupply: rental listing stock surged +21.2%, with new rental listings up +11.4%. On the construction side, September building consents jumped +27% month-on-month to 3,747 units, suggesting the development pipeline is re-accelerating even as existing inventory remains high.

全國新增掛牌量年增+5.5%，進一步推高本已偏高的待售房源。奧克蘭庫存膨脹至31週供給量，穩居買方市場。租賃市場正經歷結構性供過於求：出租房源存量激增+21.2%，新增出租掛牌量增+11.4%。建設端方面，9月建築許可環比大增+27%至3,747套，顯示開發管線正在重新加速，儘管現有庫存仍居高不下。

DEMAND: SALES RECOVER, RENTS FALL需求：成交回升，租金下滑

REINZ data confirms the volume recovery: 80,655 sales over the past year, up +10.3%. But the picture has bifurcated since: in March 2026, Canterbury sales rose +23.6% YoY (busiest March since 2021), Marlborough +18.9%, West Coast +18.6%, Southland +18.2% — the strongest regional bid in years. Meanwhile mortgage rates have re-risen to 5.25–5.29%, well above the pre-COVID norm of ~3% and rising, threatening the affordability arithmetic that drove the recent recovery. Buyer uncertainty is most acute in Auckland, where elevated inventory plus rising rates discourages aggressive bidding. The political landscape adds a layer of risk: the capital gains tax (CGT) debate has resurfaced ahead of the election, potentially dampening investor sentiment. On the positive side, the RBNZ eased LVR restrictions in December 2025, reducing deposit requirements and broadening access for first-home buyers.

REINZ數據確認成交量回升：過去一年共80,655宗成交，年增+10.3%。但從那之後格局已分化：2026 年 3 月，坎特伯雷成交年增 +23.6%（2021 年以來最忙的 3 月）、馬爾堡 +18.9%、西海岸 +18.6%、南地 +18.2%，區域市場買盤是多年來最強。同時房貸利率已回升至 5.25–5.29%，遠高於疫情前約 3% 的常態且仍在上行，威脅近期復甦所仰賴的可負擔性算式。買家不確定性在奧克蘭最為明顯，高庫存疊加房貸利率回升抑制積極出價。政治面增添風險層：資本利得稅（CGT）議題在大選前重新浮現，可能打壓投資者情緒。正面因素方面，RBNZ於2025年12月放寬LVR限制，降低首付要求，擴大首購族入場渠道。

VALUATION: CITY DIVERGENCE估值：城市分化

The national median of NZ$786,977 produces a gross yield of 4.12% against the OCR of 2.25%, a positive spread of +1.87% — the widest in years. But this OCR-based spread flatters the picture: actual leveraged investors borrow at the 2-yr fixed of 5.25–5.29%, so the operative carry against borrowing cost is -1.13% to -1.17% (negative). Auckland at NZ$1.014M yields just 3.96%, well below the 5.25%+ mortgage rate — leveraged investors face deeply negative carry. Auckland remains -18% from its 2021 peak. Christchurch at NZ$697,000 delivers 4.8% gross, with select suburbs reaching 5.5–6%, offering the closest-to-breakeven risk-adjusted carry in the country.

全國中位價NZ$786,977對應毛收益率4.12%，扣除OCR 2.25%後利差+1.87%——多年來最寬。但此 OCR 基礎之利差美化了實況：實際槓桿投資人以 2 年期固定5.25–5.29%借款，相對借款成本的有效持有差為-1.13% 至 -1.17%（負）。奧克蘭NZ$101.4萬收益率僅3.96%，遠低於 5.25%+ 房貸利率——槓桿投資人面臨深度負持有差，且距 2021 年高峰仍跌-18%。基督城NZ$69.7萬毛收益率4.8%，部分郊區達5.5至6%，是全國最接近損益兩平的風險調整後持有差。

AUCKLAND: HIGH INVENTORY SUPPRESSION奧克蘭：高庫存壓制

Auckland's median peaked at NZ$1.35M in late 2021 and has since corrected -24.9% to NZ$1.014M. Inventory sits at 31 weeks of supply, the highest among major cities and well into buyer's market territory. South Auckland offers entry-level opportunities, but the apartment segment faces acute oversupply with new builds competing against existing stock. Net migration, which surged to 30% of Auckland's population growth, is moderating — removing a key demand pillar. Recovery depends on inventory normalizing below 20 weeks, which is unlikely before late 2026 at current absorption rates.

奧克蘭中位價在2021年末觸及NZ$135萬高峰，此後累計下跌-24.9%至NZ$101.4萬。庫存高達31週，為各主要城市之最，深陷買方市場。南奧克蘭提供入門級機會，但公寓板塊面臨嚴重供過於求——新建項目與存量房源直接競爭。淨移民曾佔奧克蘭人口增長的30%，如今正在放緩，削弱了一根重要的需求支柱。復甦取決於庫存正常化至20週以下，按當前去化速度，最早2026年末方可實現。

CHRISTCHURCH: STRONGEST RESILIENCE基督城：最強韌性

Christchurch stands apart as New Zealand's most resilient market, underpinned by the post-2011 earthquake rebuild that created one of the youngest housing stocks in the country. The median of NZ$697,000 is 31% cheaper than Auckland and has risen +4.03% year-on-year, sitting just $4,000 below its all-time peak. Six suburbs — Shirley, Addington, Sydenham, Riccarton, Papanui, and Halswell — deliver gross yields of 4.3–6%, the best in the country. The key risk is earthquake insurance cost, which remains elevated and can erode net returns by 0.5–1% annually.

基督城是紐西蘭最具韌性的市場，得益於2011年地震後重建所形成的全國最年輕住宅存量。中位價NZ$69.7萬較奧克蘭便宜31%，年漲+4.03%，距歷史高峰僅差$4,000。六個郊區——Shirley、Addington、Sydenham、Riccarton、Papanui和Halswell——毛收益率達4.3至6%，為全國之最。主要風險是地震保險成本偏高，可年削淨回報0.5至1%。

POLICY: FOREIGN BAN / DTI / LVR / CGT政策：禁外購 / DTI / LVR / CGT

New Zealand has the strictest foreign buyer regime in the OECD. The Overseas Investment Amendment Act 2018 (effective 22 Oct 2018) classified residential land as "sensitive," effectively banning non-resident foreigners from purchasing existing homes outright. Only permanent residents, Australian citizens, and Singaporean citizens (under the 2016 Singapore FTA) retain full purchase rights. Non-residents may buy new apartment developments only under specific hotel-style / build-to-rent carve-outs. Pre-2018, foreign buyers accounted for ~3% of national transactions (higher in Auckland Central at ~10%). Post-ban, the number is <0.5% nationally.

Macroprudential stack: LVR (Loan-to-Value Ratio) restrictions cap owner-occupier lending at 80% LTV for >20% of a bank's new lending (eased Dec 2025), and investor lending at 70% LTV for >5% of new lending. DTI (Debt-to-Income) limits were introduced July 2024 — banks must limit owner-occupier lending at DTI >6x to <20% of new flow, investor at DTI >7x to <20%. Capital gains tax: NZ has no general CGT (only the 10-year bright-line test for residential property, tightened to 5 years under the 2024 government reversal, with intent-based deemed sales). The CGT debate remains a tail risk: Labour-led coalitions have repeatedly tabled CGT proposals, though the current National-led government has shelved them through 2026.

紐西蘭擁有OECD最嚴格的外資買家制度。2018年海外投資修訂法（2018年10月22日生效）將住宅土地分類為「敏感資產」，實質禁止非居民外國人購買既有住宅。僅永久居民、澳洲公民、新加坡公民（依2016年星紐FTA）保留完整購買權。非居民僅可在特定飯店式/建租型豁免下購買新公寓開發。2018年前，外資買家佔全國交易約3%（奧克蘭中心區約10%）。禁令後降至全國<0.5%。

宏觀審慎堆疊：LVR（貸款成數）限制自住者按揭80%LTV佔銀行新貸款>20%（2025年12月放寬），投資者70%LTV佔新貸款>5%。DTI（負債所得比）限制於2024年7月引入——銀行自住者DTI>6倍須佔新貸款流<20%，投資者DTI>7倍<20%。資本利得稅：紐西蘭無一般CGT（僅住宅物業10年明線測試，2024年政府逆轉收緊至5年、含意圖基礎擬制銷售）。CGT議題仍為尾部風險：工黨聯盟曾多次提出CGT方案，但現任國家黨政府2026年前已擱置。

OUTLOOK AND KEY RISKS展望與主要風險

Bull case (+3–6%, prob 25%): Middle East tensions de-escalate, inflation cools, RBNZ holds at 2.25% through 2026, mortgage rates drift back toward 4.5%. Inventory normalizes; Christchurch breaks to new highs, Auckland recovers 3–5%, regional markets continue +8–12%.

Base case (-1 to +2%, prob 45%): ANZ/ASB/Infometrics consensus plays out — RBNZ delivers two hikes in H2 2026 to 2.75%, mortgage rates stay at 5.25–5.50%. Volume recovery stalls; regional markets give back gains; Auckland flat to -2%, Christchurch +2–3%, regionals +3–6% (off elevated base).

Bear case (-4 to -7%, prob 30%): Iran shock persists, headline CPI re-accelerates past 4%; RBNZ delivers three hikes to 3.00% (or front-loads at May 27); CGT debate intensifies pre-election. Mortgage rates push to 5.75–6.0%. Auckland -7–10% (negative wealth feedback), Christchurch -2–4%, regionals -3–6%.

Key variables to monitor: 27 May 2026 RBNZ decision (imminent — first chance to hike), CPI prints (Q2 release dictates July hike), 2-yr swap rate (banks pre-position via wholesale), CGT/election positioning, and net migration.
