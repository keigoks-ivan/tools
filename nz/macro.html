Monetary Cycle × House Prices貨幣週期 × 房價
What to watch: New Zealand was the first OECD central bank to hike in Oct 2021 — 7 months ahead of the Fed. This early-mover advantage meant NZ also entered and exited the correction cycle ahead of peers. The HPI drop of −20.8% peak-to-trough is the deepest in the developed world for this cycle (exceeding Sweden −20% and Canada −18%), reflecting NZ's extreme valuation and short-term fixed mortgage structure (most mortgages refix every 1–2 years). RBNZ is now cutting aggressively — OCR at 3.50% (Apr 2025), with market pricing a further 25–50bp.
重點觀察：紐西蘭於2021年10月成為首個升息的OECD央行，比聯準會早7個月。這一先行優勢意味著紐西蘭也比同儕更早進入並走出修正週期。HPI峰谷跌幅20.8%是本輪週期中已開發國家最深的跌幅（超過瑞典-20%和加拿大-18%），反映了紐西蘭極端的估值水位和短期固定利率房貸結構（多數房貸每1–2年重新定價）。RBNZ目前正積極降息——2025年4月OCR達3.50%，市場預期還有25–50bp降息空間。

Real Economy × House Prices實體經濟 × 房價
What to watch: New Zealand's housing market is hyper-sensitive to net migration — the country has only 5M people and immigration swings of 50–80k/yr represent 1–2% of population. The COVID border closure (2020–22) caused a net migration deficit that paradoxically amplified the price boom (domestic demand with no new supply). The reopening surge of 130k+ net arrivals (2023) then competed with sharply higher mortgage rates, creating a "collision" — prices fell despite population growth, as affordability broke.
重點觀察：紐西蘭房市對淨移民數量極為敏感——全國人口僅500萬，每年5–8萬人的移民波動相當於人口的1–2%。COVID邊境關閉（2020–22年）造成淨移民赤字，矛盾的是反而加劇了房價繁榮（內需強勁但無新供給）。邊境重開後2023年淨移民激增逾13萬人，卻與急速上升的房貸利率相遇，形成「碰撞」——可負擔性崩潰使房價在人口增長的背景下仍然下跌。

2008–2009 · GFC SHOCK2008–2009 · GFC衝擊
RBNZ Cuts 575bp — HPI Falls 10%RBNZ降息575bp，HPI跌10%
RBNZ cuts OCR aggressively from 8.25% to 2.50% — 575bp in 12 months. GDP contracts −2.1% in 2009. National house prices fall ~10% peak to trough. Canterbury earthquakes (Sep 2010, Feb 2011) destroy 10,000+ homes, creating acute Christchurch housing shortage. Recovery begins 2010 with rate stabilisation.RBNZ大幅降息，12個月內從8.25%降至2.50%，降幅575bp。2009年GDP衰退2.1%，全國房價峰谷跌約10%。坎特伯雷地震（2010年9月、2011年2月）摧毀逾1萬套房屋，造成基督城急性住房短缺。2010年利率穩定後展開復甦。
2010–2014 · RECOVERY BOOM2010–2014 · 復甦榮景
Auckland Prices Surge; LVR Restrictions Begin奧克蘭房價急漲，LVR限制開始實施
Auckland rapidly emerges as OECD's most unaffordable city. Net migration accelerates to record +69k (2014). RBNZ introduces loan-to-value restrictions (LVR) in Oct 2013 — first central bank in world to use macroprudential tools at scale. OCR hiked 100bp (2.50%→3.50%) in 2014. HPI rises +45% nationally 2010–14, Auckland +70%.奧克蘭迅速成為OECD可負擔性最差的城市。淨移民加速至創紀錄的6.9萬人（2014年）。RBNZ於2013年10月引入貸款價值比（LVR）限制——成為全球首個大規模使用宏觀審慎工具的央行。2014年OCR升息100bp（2.50%→3.50%）。全國HPI於2010–14年累漲45%，奧克蘭漲70%。
2018 · FOREIGN BUYER BAN2018年 · 外國買家禁令
Overseas Investment Amendment Act — No Foreign Buyers境外投資修正法令禁止外國人置業
Labour government bans most foreign nationals from purchasing existing homes (Oct 2018) — one of the most radical demand-side interventions globally. HPI cools sharply: Auckland −4% in 2018. Capital gains tax proposal (later abandoned) further dampens sentiment. RBNZ holds OCR at 1.75% as CPI stays subdued. Migration running at +50k/yr keeps long-term demand floor.工黨政府禁止大多數外國人購買現有住宅（2018年10月）——全球最激進的需求側干預之一。HPI急速降溫：奧克蘭2018年跌4%。資本利得稅提案（後被放棄）進一步打壓市場情緒。RBNZ維持OCR在1.75%，CPI持續溫和。每年逾5萬人的移民為長期需求提供支撐。
MAR 2020 · COVID EMERGENCY CUT2020年3月 · COVID緊急降息
OCR to Historic Low 0.25%; QE + FLPOCR降至歷史低點0.25%；QE+FLP
RBNZ cuts emergency 75bp to 0.25% in Mar 2020. Launches NZ$100bn Large-Scale Asset Purchase (LSAP) program. Funding for Lending Programme (FLP) gives banks access to cheap RBNZ funding at 0.25%. Borders close — immigration plunges from +50k to near zero. Counter-intuitively, house prices rocket: GDP rebounds +5.7% in 2021; fiscal stimulus (Wage Subsidy Scheme) prevents income loss; ultra-cheap credit floods property.RBNZ於2020年3月緊急降息75bp至0.25%，啟動1000億紐元大規模資產購買計劃（LSAP）。融資貸款計劃（FLP）使銀行以0.25%的極低成本從RBNZ融資。邊境關閉，移民從5萬多人驟降至近零。但房價卻反直覺地大幅上漲：2021年GDP反彈5.7%；財政刺激（薪資補貼計劃）防止收入損失；超廉價信貸大舉湧入房市。
2020–2021 · PANDEMIC PRICE BOOM2020–2021 · 疫情房價榮景
National HPI +31% in 12 Months — OECD Record全國HPI 12個月+31%，創OECD紀錄
Peak annual HPI growth of +31.1% in Oct 2021 — among the highest ever recorded in the OECD. Median Auckland house price hits NZ$1.3M. RBNZ temporarily removes LVR restrictions (May 2020, restored March 2021). Government's Bright-Line Test extended to 10 years (Mar 2021) to cool investment demand. Investor interest-only lending tightened. First-home buyer share hits 30% — FOMO at peak.HPI年漲幅於2021年10月達+31.1%峰值——OECD史上最高紀錄之一。奧克蘭房屋中位價達130萬紐元。RBNZ暫時取消LVR限制（2020年5月，2021年3月恢復）。政府將光明線測試延長至10年（2021年3月）以冷卻投資需求。首次置業者比例達30%，FOMO情緒見頂。
OCT 2021 · FIRST HIKE — 7 MONTHS AHEAD OF FED2021年10月 · 首次升息——比聯準會提前7個月
RBNZ Hikes 525bp: 0.25%→5.50% (2021–2023)RBNZ升息525bp：0.25%→5.50%（2021–2023）
RBNZ first G10 central bank to hike post-COVID — Oct 2021. 12 consecutive hikes to 5.50% by May 2023. 1-yr fixed mortgage rate peaks at 7.4% (2023). National HPI falls −20.8% peak-to-trough (Nov 2021–Jun 2023) — deepest correction of any major market. Auckland −25%. Affordability stress: debt-service ratio hits 50%+ for recent buyers. REINZ sales volumes fall to 30-yr lows.RBNZ成為COVID後首個升息的G10央行——2021年10月。連續升息12次至2023年5月的5.50%。1年期固定房貸利率於2023年達7.4%峰值。全國HPI峰谷跌20.8%（2021年11月–2023年6月），為任何主要市場中最深的修正。奧克蘭跌25%。可負擔性壓力：近期買家的還款收入比突破50%。REINZ銷售量跌至30年低點。
2024 · AGGRESSIVE RBNZ EASING2024年 · RBNZ積極降息
200bp Cuts in 6 Months: 5.50%→3.50%6個月降息200bp：5.50%→3.50%
RBNZ pivots sharply — 50bp cuts in Oct and Nov 2024, then 25bp in Feb and Apr 2025. CPI returns to 2.5% target band. GDP weak (+0.7% 2024). Unemployment rising (5.1% Q4 2024). Government repeals foreign buyer ban (Apr 2024), allowing non-resident purchases again. HPI YoY still negative but monthly rate of decline slowing. Market expects RBNZ to reach 3.00% neutral rate by mid-2025.RBNZ急劇轉向——2024年10月及11月各降息50bp，2025年2月及4月各降25bp。CPI回落至2.5%目標帶。GDP疲弱（2024年+0.7%）。失業率上升（2024年Q4達5.1%）。政府廢除外國買家禁令（2024年4月），允許非居民再度購屋。HPI年率仍為負，但月度跌速放緩。市場預期RBNZ將於2025年中達3.00%中性利率。
2025 · TROUGH → RECOVERY TRANSITION2025年 · 底部→復甦過渡
Foreign Buyers Return; Building Consents Collapse外國買家回歸；建築許可量崩跌
Government scrapped foreign buyer ban (Apr 2024) — first Asian buyers (Singapore, Hong Kong, Taiwan) returning, particularly in Auckland and Queenstown. Building consents collapsed −25% in 2024 — lowest in 10 years, constraining future supply. Mortgage rates falling (1-yr fixed at 5.8% and declining). Net migration moderating to ~40k (2025 est.). Market expected to transition from correction to early recovery H2 2025.政府廢除外國買家禁令（2024年4月）——首批亞洲買家（新加坡、香港、台灣）回歸，尤其是奧克蘭和皇后鎮。2024年建築許可量崩跌25%——10年新低，限制未來供給。房貸利率下行（1年期固定利率降至5.8%且仍在下行）。淨移民趨緩至約4萬人（2025年估）。市場預期於2025年下半年從修正過渡至早期復甦。
