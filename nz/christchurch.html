SUMMARY市場概述

Post-Earthquake Recovery Complete震後復甦完成

Christchurch has emerged from the 2011 earthquake rebuild as New Zealand's strongest major market. The median price of NZ$697,000 sits just $4,000 below its 2022 peak — the best recovery of any NZ city. Affordability relative to Auckland (31% discount) combined with high yields in suburbs like Riccarton and Addington (5–6%) make it the standout value play. The modern rebuilt housing stock, steady population growth through domestic and international migration, and a diversified economy anchored by agriculture, technology, and education underpin sustained demand.基督城已從2011年地震重建中崛起，成為紐西蘭表現最強的主要市場。中位價NZ$697,000僅比2022年高峰低$4,000——為紐西蘭各城市中最佳復甦表現。相對奧克蘭的可負擔性（折價31%）加上瑞卡頓和艾丁頓等郊區的高收益率（5-6%），使其成為突出的價值型投資標的。現代化的重建住宅存量、透過國內和國際移民帶動的穩定人口增長，以及以農業、科技和教育為支柱的多元化經濟，共同支撐持續性需求。
