SUMMARY總結

High Inventory高庫存

At 31 weeks of inventory, Auckland is firmly in buyer's market territory. Sellers face extended listing times and increasing price negotiation pressure.庫存達31週，奧克蘭已穩居買方市場。賣方面臨更長的掛牌時間與日益增大的議價壓力。

Price Correction價格修正

Prices remain 24.9% below the 2022 peak of NZ$1.35M. The correction has been orderly but prolonged, with no clear catalyst for a sharp recovery.房價仍較2022年高峰NZ$1.35M低24.9%。修正有序但持續，目前看不到急速復甦的明確催化劑。

Yield Recovery收益率回升

As prices have fallen while rents held steady, gross yields have improved to 3.96%. Further OCR cuts could widen the yield spread for investors.房價下跌而租金持穩，毛收益率改善至3.96%。進一步降息可望擴大投資者利差。

First-Home Opportunity首購機會

South and West Auckland offer entry points at NZ$700–900K. Combined with OCR cuts improving borrowing capacity, first-home buyers have the best window in years.南區和西區奧克蘭提供NZ$70-90萬的入場機會。加上降息改善借貸能力，首購族迎來多年來最佳窗口。
