Housing for All (2021-2030)全民住房計劃 (2021-2030)

The government's flagship housing plan targets 33,000 completions per year by 2030. Progress has been mixed: while completions approach target, social and affordable housing delivery lags. The plan faces risks from judicial reviews delaying large-scale developments, construction cost inflation, and labour shortages in the building sector.政府旗艦住房計劃目標至2030年每年完工33,000戶。進展參差不齊：雖然完工量接近目標，但社會住宅和可負擔住宅交付落後。計劃面臨司法審查延遲大型開發案、建造成本通膨、建築業勞動力短缺等風險。

Key Risk Factors關鍵風險因素

Planning delays: Judicial reviews add 12-18 months to large projects. Construction costs: Up 42% since 2019, squeezing viability. Tech sector: Ireland's reliance on multinational tech employers creates concentration risk. RPZ caps: The 2% annual rent increase limit may discourage new rental investment, further tightening supply.規劃延遲：司法審查為大型項目增加12-18個月。建造成本：較2019年上漲42%，壓縮可行性。科技業：愛爾蘭依賴跨國科技企業帶來集中風險。RPZ上限：年租金漲幅2%限制可能抑制新租賃投資，進一步緊縮供給。
