Monetary Cycle × House Prices貨幣週期 × 房價
What to watch: Ireland shares ECB monetary policy with 19 other eurozone nations — it cannot set rates independently. This means Irish property absorbed a decade of near-zero ECB rates despite domestic prices already surging, amplifying the boom. The 2022–23 ECB hike to 4.50% barely dented Irish prices because supply is so constrained (completions vs. household formation gap of ~20K/yr).
重點觀察：愛爾蘭與19個歐元區國家共用ECB貨幣政策，無法自行設定利率。這意味著愛爾蘭房市在國內房價已急漲的情況下，仍吸收了十年近零利率的刺激，放大了繁榮程度。2022–23年ECB升息至4.50%幾乎未能壓制愛爾蘭房價，因為供給嚴重不足（每年住宅落成量vs家庭形成數差距約2萬套）。

Real Economy × House Prices實體經濟 × 房價
What to watch: Ireland's official GDP is distorted by multinational tax arrangements — Modified GNI (GNI*) is the better domestic measure. Employment has been the strongest prop for housing: unemployment fell from 15.1% (2012) to 4.4% (2025), directly expanding the mortgage-eligible population despite affordability stress.
重點觀察：愛爾蘭官方GDP因跨國企業稅務安排而失真——修正後GNI（GNI*）是更佳的國內衡量指標。就業是支撐房市的最強支柱：失業率從2012年的15.1%降至2025年的4.4%，直接擴大了可申請按揭的人口基礎，儘管可負擔性仍面臨壓力。

Key Cycle Events (2008–2025)關鍵週期事件（2008–2025）
2008–2012 · THE CRASH2008–2012 · 大崩盤
RPPI Falls −54% — Worst in Developed WorldRPPI暴跌−54%，發達國家最慘
Celtic Tiger property bubble bursts. GDP contracts −5.5% in 2009. Bank sector collapses — Anglo Irish Bank, Allied Irish Banks receive state bailout. Unemployment surges 5%→15%. Ireland requests EU/IMF bailout (€85bn, Nov 2010). Negative equity traps 200,000+ households.凱爾特之虎房產泡沫破裂，2009年GDP衰退5.5%。銀行業崩潰——盎格魯愛爾蘭銀行、聯合愛爾蘭銀行獲政府救助。失業率從5%飆升至15%。2010年11月愛爾蘭申請EU/IMF救助（850億歐元）。超過20萬戶家庭陷入負資產困境。
2009–2014 · NAMA ESTABLISHED2009–2014 · NAMA成立
Bad Bank Absorbs €74bn in Distressed Loans壞帳銀行吸收740億歐元不良貸款
National Asset Management Agency (NAMA) takes over toxic property loans from Irish banks at discount. Manages wind-down of distressed assets 2009–2020. Enables gradual price stabilisation. Exits most positions by 2017 with eventual profit of ~€4bn — rare success among bad banks globally.全國資產管理局（NAMA）以折扣價從愛爾蘭銀行接管有毒房產貸款。2009–2020年管理不良資產清算，幫助房價逐步穩定。2017年前清退大部分倉位，最終獲利約40億歐元——全球壞帳銀行中罕見的成功案例。
2013–2017 · RECOVERY BEGINS2013–2017 · 復甦啟動
MNC FDI Boom + Dublin Property Surge跨國企業FDI榮景+都柏林房價急升
Google, Facebook, Apple, LinkedIn establish European HQs in Dublin. Tech employment booms. ECB holds rates near zero (0.05% by 2014). RPPI rebounds — Dublin leads, up 50%+ from trough by 2017. CSO introduces Central Bank LTV/LTI lending limits in 2015 to cool excesses.谷歌、臉書、蘋果、領英在都柏林設立歐洲總部，科技就業大幅擴張。ECB利率接近零（2014年降至0.05%）。RPPI反彈——都柏林領漲，到2017年較谷底回升逾50%。2015年央行引入LTV/LTI貸款上限以抑制過熱。
2018–2019 · SUPPLY CONSTRAINT CRISIS2018–2019 · 供給短缺危機
Completions Only 18K/yr vs. 35K+ Needed每年落成僅1.8萬套，需求超3.5萬套
Construction capacity destroyed during crash fails to recover fast enough. Homelessness reaches record 10,500. Government launches Rebuilding Ireland action plan (2016). Dublin rents +50% vs 2012. Foreign institutional funds (REITs, cuckoo funds) bulk-buy new builds — political controversy erupts.崩盤期間建築業產能被摧毀，復甦遲緩。無家可歸人數創新高達10,500人。政府推出「重建愛爾蘭」行動計劃（2016年）。都柏林租金較2012年上漲50%。外資機構基金（REITs、布穀鳥基金）批量購入新樓——政治爭議爆發。
2020–2021 · COVID — PRICES HOLD2020–2021 · COVID房價撐住
ECB Emergency PEPP + Construction StopsECB緊急PEPP+建築業停擺
GDP falls −5.5% but housing completions halt (construction sites closed). Pent-up demand accumulates. ECB's €1.85tn PEPP keeps rates at zero. RPPI flat in 2020 then explodes +7% in 2021. WFH triggers demand for houses over apartments. Cork, Galway, Limerick join the surge.GDP衰退5.5%，但住宅落成停擺（工地關閉），積壓需求累積。ECB 1.85兆歐元PEPP維持零利率。RPPI 2020年持平，2021年爆漲+7%。在家工作帶動對獨立屋需求，科克、戈爾韋、利默里克跟進上漲。
JUL 2022 – SEP 2023 · ECB HIKE CYCLE2022年7月–2023年9月 · ECB升息週期
10 Hikes: 0% → 4.50% — Supply Still the Binding Constraint10次升息：0% → 4.50%，供給仍是主要約束
Fastest ECB tightening since euro creation. Variable mortgage costs soar for Irish households. Yet RPPI keeps rising: +6.5% (2022), +2.5% (2023). Supply shortage overwhelms rate effect. New build completions reach 29K in 2022 — still far short of demand. Government bans institutional bulk-buying of new houses.歐元誕生以來ECB最快緊縮。愛爾蘭家庭浮動利率房貸成本飆升。然而RPPI仍持續上漲：2022年+6.5%，2023年+2.5%。供給短缺壓倒利率效果。2022年新屋落成達2.9萬套，仍遠低於需求。政府禁止機構批量購入新建住宅。
2024 · ECB CUTS + PRICES HIT ATH2024年 · ECB降息+房價創歷史新高
RPPI +9.8% YoY — Help-to-Buy Scheme ExtendedRPPI年增+9.8%，購屋補助計劃延長
ECB starts cutting June 2024. Irish RPPI surges as lower rates + persistent supply deficit combine. Dublin median house exceeds €400K. Help-to-Buy scheme extended to 2029. Government Housing for All plan targets 33K completions/yr — achieved partially. Net immigration +80K fuels continued demand.ECB於2024年6月開始降息。愛爾蘭RPPI急漲，利率下降加上持續供給缺口雙重驅動。都柏林房價中位數突破40萬歐元。購屋補助計劃延長至2029年。政府「全民住房」計劃目標每年3.3萬套落成——部分達成。淨移入8萬人持續帶動需求。
2025 · LATE CYCLE — POLICY RESPONSE2025年 · 週期末段——政策回應
Stamp Duty on Bulk Purchases; Affordability at Crisis Level批量購屋印花稅；可負擔性達危機水準
Government introduces 15% stamp duty on bulk purchases (10+ units) from 2024. Price-to-income ratio in Dublin reaches 9–11× — worst in EU outside Luxembourg. ECB deposit rate at 2.65% (Apr 2025). Completions projected 33K in 2025. Housing remains top political priority after 2025 general election.政府對批量購屋（10套以上）徵收15%印花稅（自2024年起）。都柏林房價所得比達9–11倍——為盧森堡以外歐盟最高。ECB存款利率2.65%（2025年4月）。2025年預計落成3.3萬套。住房問題在2025年大選後仍是最優先政治議題。
