SUMMARY摘要

Price Trend價格趨勢

Prices continued to rise through Q1 2026 driven by chronic undersupply relative to demand. Dublin leads gains at +8.1% while regional cities follow at 5-7%.受長期供不應求影響，2026年第一季房價持續上漲。都柏林漲幅領先達8.1%，地方城市介於5-7%。

Rental Crisis租賃危機

Ireland's rental market remains among the tightest in Europe. National vacancy at 0.7% continues to push rents higher despite RPZ caps of 2% per year.愛爾蘭租賃市場仍為歐洲最緊縮之一。全國空置率0.7%持續推升租金，儘管RPZ年漲幅上限為2%。

Supply Gap供給缺口

Completions of 32,695 units narrowly missed the government's 33,000 target. The construction sector faces labour shortages and rising material costs.全年完工32,695戶，略低於政府33,000戶目標。營建業面臨勞動力短缺與材料成本上升。

Population Pressure人口壓力

Net migration of +68,900 and natural population growth push Ireland's population to 5.38M, the highest since pre-famine levels, amplifying housing demand.淨移民+68,900人加上自然增長，將愛爾蘭人口推至5.38M，為大飢荒前以來最高，加劇住房需求。
