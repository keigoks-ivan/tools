⚠ Tax red flags before you commit投資前必看的稅務地雷

  • Worldwide income tax exposure. Becoming tax resident in many of these countries means your global income is taxed there. Turkey, Greece, Hungary, Malta — all worldwide taxation if you live ≥183 days/year.全球所得稅暴露。取得這些國家稅務居民身份意味著全球所得在當地課稅。土耳其、希臘、匈牙利、馬爾他 — 居住 ≥183 天就要全球課稅。
  • Exit tax on unrealized gains. If you take residency in a country and later move, several jurisdictions tax unrealized capital gains as if you sold (e.g. some EU members).離境未實現利得稅。取得居留後再離開，部分國家會把未實現資本利得視同已賣出課稅（部分歐盟國家）。
  • Property minimum ≠ visa cost. Add: legal fees (1–7%), foreign-buyer stamp duty (varies), application fees ($5K–25K typical), agency commissions. Real out-of-pocket can be 15–25% above the headline number.房產門檻 ≠ 簽證總成本。還要加：律師費（1–7%）、外國買家印花稅、申請費（$5K–25K）、仲介佣金。實際支出常比標題數字高 15–25%。
  • Holding period before resale. Most programs require 3–7 years property holding before you can sell without losing the visa. Don't expect to flip.賣出前持有期限。多數計畫要求持有 3–7 年才能賣不失去身份。不要把這當短炒。
  • Citizenship vs PR is a meaningful difference. Turkey/Malta give a passport. Greece/Hungary/UAE/MM2H give residency only — citizenship comes after 7–10 years living + tax residency tests.公民 vs PR 有實質差別。土耳其／馬爾他給護照。希臘／匈牙利／UAE／MM2H 只給居留 — 公民身份要再 7–10 年居住＋稅務居民測試。
