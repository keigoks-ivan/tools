How We Compile This Data資料如何編製

Every number on Property Monitor traces back to a primary statistical agency, central bank, or major listings dataset. This page lists every source by market, defines each metric, and documents how derived figures (carry spread, PTI, P/R) are calculated. Property Monitor 上每個數字都可追溯到一手統計機構、央行或主要房源資料庫。本頁依市場列出所有資料來源，定義每個指標，並記錄衍生數字（利差、房價收入比、房租比）的計算方式。

Update cadence:更新週期： Macro figures (GDP, CPI, central bank rates) refreshed when officially released. Price indices refreshed quarterly. Vacancy & pipeline reviewed every 3 months. Foreign-buyer tax tables reviewed when policy changes are announced. 總體指標（GDP、CPI、央行利率）依官方公告更新。房價指數每季更新。空置率與在建供給每 3 個月複核。外國買家稅表於政策變動公告時更新。

Disclaimer:免責聲明： All data on Property Monitor is for informational purposes only and does not constitute investment, tax, legal, or financial advice. Always verify figures with the original source and a qualified local advisor before making any property investment decision. Property Monitor 所有資料僅供參考，不構成投資、稅務、法律或財務建議。任何房產投資決策前請以一手來源資料及合格在地顧問為準。
DERIVED METRIC FORMULAS衍生指標計算公式
Carry Spread
Gross rental yield − prevailing local mortgage rate. Positive = rent covers debt service. 毛租金收益率 − 當地現行房貸利率。正值代表租金可負擔利息。
+2% income · 0~+2% neutral · -1~0% mild · <-3% deep negative +2% 收租 · 0~+2% 中性 · -1~0% 輕負 · <-3% 深負
PTI — Price-to-Income
Median apartment price ÷ median annual household income. 中位公寓房價 ÷ 中位家庭年收入。
<5× affordable · 5–10× stretched · >10× severely unaffordable <5 倍可負擔 · 5–10 倍偏高 · >10 倍嚴重不可負擔
P/R — Price-to-Rent
Median price ÷ gross annual rent of comparable unit. 中位房價 ÷ 同類單位毛年租。
<20× buy favored · 20–30× neutral · >30× rent favored <20 倍買有利 · 20–30 倍中性 · >30 倍租有利
Vacancy Rate
Rental vacancy rate (units listed but not let). Where unavailable, listings-to-stock ratio used as proxy. 租賃空置率（已掛牌但未出租單位佔比）。無法取得時以掛牌量／存量為替代指標。
<1% crisis tight · 1–2% tight · 2–3.5% balanced · >3.5% soft <1% 危機緊張 · 1–2% 緊張 · 2–3.5% 平衡 · >3.5% 鬆軟
Pipeline %
Units under construction (approved & commenced) as percentage of existing housing stock. 在建單位（已核准且開工）佔現有住宅存量百分比。
Low <1% · Mod 1–2.5% · High >2.5% 低 <1% · 中 1–2.5% · 高 >2.5%
10yr Cumulative
Apartment price index level Q1 2026 ÷ Q1 2016 − 100%. Local currency, not USD. 公寓房價指數 2026Q1 ÷ 2016Q1 − 100%。當地貨幣計，非美元。
Sources: country price index for the apartment segment specifically 來源：各國公寓區段專屬房價指數
SSD — Seller Stamp Duty
Punitive duty on short-hold sales (Singapore: 12% if <1yr, tapering to 0% after 3yr). 短期持有出售懲罰性印花稅（新加坡：<1 年 12%，3 年後降至 0%）。
FIRPTA
US Foreign Investment in Real Property Tax Act — 15% withholding on gross sale price by non-resident sellers. 美國《外國人投資不動產稅法》— 非居民賣方按總售價預扣 15%。
CGT Cliff
Sharp drop in capital gains tax rate after a holding-period threshold. MY: 30%→10% at yr 6. TW: 45%→20% at yr 5. JP: 39%→20% at yr 5. 資本利得稅率在持有年限門檻後驟降。馬來西亞：第 6 年 30%→10%。台灣：第 5 年 45%→20%。日本：第 5 年 39%→20%。
LTV Cap
Maximum loan-to-value ratio. Foreign-buyer LTV is often capped lower than domestic (e.g. TW 2nd home 30–60%). 最高貸款成數。外國買家 LTV 上限通常低於本地（如台灣第二戶 30–60%）。
