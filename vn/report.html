EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

Vietnam's residential property market entered Q2 2026 in an unusual late-cycle phase: strong price momentum in Hanoi and HCMC supported by severe supply scarcity, but with the easy-gains phase of the post-COVID recovery clearly fading. Hanoi primary-market apartments averaged approximately US$2,865/m² in Q1 2025 and pushed past US$3,100/m² through 2026 with city-wide growth of +7 to +12% YoY, led by the western corridor (Nam Tu Liem, Cau Giay). HCMC primary averaged US$3,316/m² in early 2025, rising +4 to +9% YoY to over US$3,500/m² as cautious supply recovery began. Per-unit, a family-sized apartment in either city now costs VND 5–7 billion (US$195,000–275,000).

The structural tension sits between capital and credit. FDI disbursement reached a five-year high of approximately US$23.6 billion in the first 11 months of 2025, up +8.9% YoY, and macro growth remains at 6–7%, anchoring medium-term housing demand. But mortgage rates remain elevated at approximately 9%, constraining domestic affordability, and the SBV has signalled tighter control over capital flows into real estate in its 2026 credit guidance — intended to prevent the speculative excesses seen in the 2020–2022 cycle. The result: price growth is genuine but credit rationing caps the pace.

Foreign buying is the key marginal demand channel. The 30% foreign ownership cap per building is already binding in many prime central projects, with over 2,800 foreign apartment purchases recorded in Hanoi in 2024 alone — more than the 2018–2022 five-year total combined. Korean, Chinese, Taiwanese, and Singaporean capital dominates the foreign flow, attracted by gross yields of 5–7% in Hanoi and 4–6% in HCMC against yen-like levels of yield compression elsewhere in Asia. Vietnam is the highest-growth frontier market in APAC real estate; it is also the one where execution risk is material.

越南住宅房市進入 2026 年第二季時，處於一個不尋常的「晚週期」：河內與胡志明市靠供給嚴重稀缺撐住強勁的價格動能，但疫情後復甦最輕鬆的那段漲幅已經結束。河內一手公寓 2025 年第一季均價約 US$2,865/m²，2026 年推進到 US$3,100/m² 以上，全市漲幅 +7 至 +12%，由西側走廊（南慈廉、紙橋）領漲。胡志明市一手公寓 2025 年初均價 US$3,316/m²，漲幅 +4 至 +9%，隨著供給小心復甦已突破 US$3,500/m²。以單位計，兩個城市的家庭型公寓總價都在 VND 50–70 億（美金 19.5 萬至 27.5 萬）。

結構性張力來自資金與信貸的拉鋸。2025 年前 11 個月 FDI 到位金額達五年新高約 US$236 億、年增 8.9%，總體經濟成長維持在 6–7%，撐住中期住房需求。但房貸利率仍在 9% 左右，壓縮國內負擔能力；越南國家銀行（SBV）2026 年信貸指引更明確加強對房地產資金流向的控管，目的是防止 2020–2022 年循環那種投機失控。結果就是：房價漲幅是真的，但信貸配給讓節奏不會太快。

外資是最關鍵的邊際需求。每棟建築 30% 外資持有上限在許多核心精華案已經觸頂，光是河內 2024 年就有超過 2,800 件外國人購屋，比 2018 至 2022 年五年加總還多。韓國、中國、台灣、新加坡資金主導外資流入，被河內 5–7%、胡志明市 4–6% 的毛租金收益率吸引——相較亞洲其他地方的收益率壓縮，這裡仍屬高檔。越南是亞太房地產最高成長的前沿市場，同時也是執行風險不可忽視的那一個。

MACRO CONTEXT & GROWTH DRIVERS宏觀背景與增長驅動力

Vietnam stands out among ASEAN economies with consistent 6–7% GDP growth. The manufacturing supply-chain diversification out of China has been the single most important structural story of the past five years: Vietnam has captured meaningful electronics, textiles, and increasingly semiconductor supply-chain share, with Samsung, Intel, LG, Foxconn, and more recently Chinese EV component makers building out multi-billion-dollar footprints. FDI disbursement reached US$23.6 billion in the first 11 months of 2025, an +8.9% YoY increase, and full-year 2025 disbursement is expected to exceed US$25B — the highest on record. Export growth remains in double digits, led almost entirely by FDI enterprises.

Urbanisation is at 38%, far below regional peers (Malaysia 78%, Thailand 52%, Indonesia 58%). This is a decade-plus structural story of rural-to-urban migration, with the two primary destinations being HCMC and Hanoi. The government has clearly prioritised infrastructure buildout: HCMC Metro Line 1 opened in late 2024 and has already delivered 15–20% premiums along its corridor; Long Thanh International Airport is targeting commercial opening in late 2026, expected to be Southeast Asia's largest by passenger volume when fully built; the North–South Expressway and the high-speed rail feasibility studies have both advanced materially in 2025.

The monetary backdrop is complicated and turning hawkish. The SBV has held its refinancing rate at 4.50% and discount rate at 3.00% (unchanged since June 2023), but commercial deposit and lending rates have drifted upward under inflation and FX pressure. DBS now forecasts the SBV will raise the refinancing rate by 50 bps to 5.00% by H2 2026 (most likely Q2/Q3) to defend the dong and curb imported inflation; MUFG forecasts at least one 25 bp hike. The trigger is Vietnam's accelerating CPI — DBS has raised its 2026 average headline inflation forecast to 4.8%. The SBV's 2026 credit guidance explicitly requires banks to tighten credit flows into speculative real estate and prioritise production-sector lending. Net effect for property: mortgage rates at approximately 9% cap domestic leveraged demand and may push higher into 2026, creating a market increasingly dependent on cash buyers (both domestic HNW and foreign). The SBV system-wide credit growth target of approximately 15% is supportive in aggregate, but the distributional tilt away from real estate is a material constraint.

越南在東盟經濟體裡一枝獨秀，GDP 持續維持 6–7% 成長。過去五年最重要的結構性故事，是從中國轉移出來的製造業供應鏈：越南拿下了相當份額的電子、紡織、以及越來越多的半導體供應鏈，三星、Intel、LG、鴻海，以及近期中國電動車零件廠商都在興建數十億美元規模的產能。2025 年前 11 個月 FDI 到位金額達 US$236 億、年增 8.9%，全年預估突破 US$250 億，創歷史新高。出口成長維持雙位數，幾乎全部由 FDI 企業推動。

都市化率只有 38%，遠低於區域同儕（馬來西亞 78%、泰國 52%、印尼 58%）。這是一個十年以上的結構性「鄉下往都市」遷移故事，兩個主要目的地就是胡志明與河內。政府明確把基礎建設擺在優先：胡志明市地鐵 1 號線 2024 年底通車，沿線已經帶出 15–20% 的溢價；龍城國際機場預計 2026 年底開始商業運作，完工後將是東南亞客運量最大的機場；南北高速公路、高速鐵路的可行性研究也都在 2025 年有實質進展。

貨幣環境較複雜，且轉趨鷹派。SBV 將再融資利率維持在 4.50%、貼現率 3.00%（自 2023 年 6 月起未動），但商業銀行存放款利率已在通膨與匯率壓力下走高。DBS 現預估 SBV 將在 2026 年下半年（最可能 Q2/Q3）把再融資利率上調 50 bp 至 5.00%，以捍衛盾值並抑制輸入性通膨；MUFG 預估至少一次 25 bp 升息。觸發因素是越南通膨加速——DBS 已把 2026 年整體 CPI 預估上調至 4.8%。SBV 2026 年信貸指引明確要求銀行緊縮房地產投機性資金、優先支援生產部門。對房市的直接影響是：房貸利率約 9% 壓縮了國內槓桿需求，且 2026 年內可能進一步上行，讓市場越來越依賴現金買家（國內高淨值與外資）。SBV 整體信貸成長目標約 15% 總量上仍有支撐，但房地產被分流出去，是個實質限制。

FOREIGN OWNERSHIP FRAMEWORK外國所有權框架

Foreigners may own apartments (not landed property) under Vietnam's Housing Law regime, subject to three binding constraints: (1) the 30% per-building cap on foreign ownership, (2) 50-year leasehold renewable once for a total of 100 years, and (3) funds must be remitted from abroad and documented. The 2024 Housing Law amendments materially expanded eligible buyers (including more overseas Vietnamese categories) and eligible property types, and streamlined the purchase process — the single most pro-foreign reform since the original 2015 liberalisation.

The 30% cap is the most important binding constraint in practice. In high-demand central HCMC and Hanoi projects, the foreign quota fills on pre-launch sales, creating a two-tier secondary market: post-purchase resale between foreigners is allowed but the cap is not reset, forcing foreign sellers to target Vietnamese buyers to exit cleanly. This produces a real scarcity premium in Japanese-Korean-heavy buildings and a foreign-exit discount in marginal projects. In 2024, foreigners purchased over 2,800 apartments in Hanoi alone — more than the entire 2018–2022 five-year period combined. The Korean cohort (largest, estimated 35–40% of foreign flow), Chinese, Taiwanese, Japanese, and Singaporean buyers dominate.

The policy direction continues to favour foreign participation. The Vietnamese government sees foreign capital as a channel to absorb the oversupply residue of the 2020–2022 cycle, and the 2024 Housing Law reforms have not triggered the restrictive backlash seen in Australia, Canada, or New Zealand. Transfer of foreign-owned units to Vietnamese nationals is unrestricted, providing an effective secondary-market exit. The primary policy risk is a future tightening of the 30% cap or imposition of additional foreign buyer stamp duties — both have been discussed but neither is currently on the legislative calendar for 2026.

在越南《住房法》架構下，外國人可以持有公寓（不能持有土地），受三項限制：（1）每棟建築 30% 外資持有上限、（2）50 年租賃期可續一次（共 100 年）、（3）購屋資金必須從海外匯入並留存紀錄。2024 年住房法修正案大幅擴大了合格買家（包含更多海外越南人身份）與合格房產類型，也簡化了購買流程，是自 2015 年首次開放以來對外資最有利的改革。

實務上，30% 上限是最關鍵的限制。胡志明與河內的熱門核心案，外資配額通常在預售就被吃光，因此形成雙層二級市場：外國人之間的轉售雖然允許，但配額並不重置，所以外資賣家要乾淨退場得找越南本地買家。結果是日韓買家集中的大樓出現真實稀缺溢價，而冷門外資項目則出現「外資退場折價」。2024 年光是河內，外國人就買了超過 2,800 戶公寓，比 2018 至 2022 年五年加總還多。韓國買家最大（估計佔外資流入 35–40%），其次是中國、台灣、日本、新加坡。

政策方向仍然有利外資參與。越南政府把外資視為消化 2020–2022 年循環遺留供給的管道，2024 年修法也沒有引發澳洲、加拿大、紐西蘭那種政策反彈。外國人持有單位轉讓給越南籍買家完全不受限制，給了有效的二級市場退場機制。主要政策風險是未來可能收緊 30% 配額或開徵外國人印花稅——兩者都有討論，但都不在 2026 年立法議程上。

HO CHI MINH CITY OVERVIEW胡志明市概覽

HCMC remains the primary market for foreign investors and institutional capital in Vietnam. Primary apartment prices averaged US$3,316/m² in Q1 2025 and progressed to approximately US$3,500+/m² by early 2026 on +4 to +9% annual growth. District 1 luxury condominiums trade above US$5,800/m², with flagship projects in Ba Son and Thu Thiem pushing toward US$8,000. Thu Duc City (formerly District 2) offers US$3,800/m² with metro connectivity, and has delivered the highest dollar-per-square-metre growth of any HCMC sub-market over the past 18 months.

Supply is constrained but cautiously recovering. Approximately 8,600 units of new supply entered the market in 2025 — a modest recovery from 2023 lows, but 94% of that supply came from subsequent phases or the revival of previously stalled projects, not from new project approvals. Lengthy permit processes, tightened land banking rules, and developer financing stress have created a structural approval bottleneck that will take 2–3 years to clear. Grade B apartments dominate the future pipeline at roughly 67% of planned supply through 2027, as the mid-end segment has largely disappeared from new launches.

Rental yields in HCMC average 4–6% gross for mid-range condominiums, rising to 6–7% for serviced apartments in expatriate-heavy areas (Thao Dien, An Phu, Phu My Hung). Rental growth is concentrated in District 1, District 2/Thu Duc, and District 7, where expat corporate demand remains intact. The Metro Line 1 opening in late 2024 has boosted corridor prices by +15–20%, and further metro lines (Line 2 and Line 3) are in various stages of delivery through 2028–2030. The primary risk is SBV credit tightening reducing the speed of domestic absorption; the primary upside is the ongoing FDI manufacturing wave underpinning expat and professional-class rental demand.

胡志明市仍然是越南外資與機構資金的主要市場。2025 年第一季一手公寓均價 US$3,316/m²，2026 年初已推進到 US$3,500/m² 以上，年增 +4 至 +9%。第一郡豪華公寓均價超過 US$5,800/m²，巴森（Ba Son）、守添（Thu Thiem）的精品案更推到 US$8,000 附近。守德市（前第二郡）每平方米 US$3,800，加上地鐵通連，是過去 18 個月胡志明市各區中每平方米漲幅最大的。

供給端受限但小心復甦。2025 年新推案約 8,600 戶，比 2023 年低點回升，但其中 94% 來自既有案的後續期或復活的停擺案，不是全新審核通過的項目。冗長的許可流程、土地儲備規範收緊、以及建商融資壓力，共同造成結構性的審批瓶頸，要 2–3 年才能慢慢疏通。2027 年前的規劃供給約 67% 是 B 級公寓，中端市場在新推案裡幾乎消失。

胡志明市中端公寓毛租金收益率約 4–6%，外籍人士聚集區（草甸 Thao Dien、安富 An Phu、富美興）的服務式公寓可達 6–7%。租金漲幅集中在第一郡、第二郡／守德、第七郡，外商企業需求保持完整。2024 年底地鐵 1 號線通車帶動沿線房價 +15–20%，2 號線與 3 號線也將在 2028–2030 年間分階段完成。主要風險是 SBV 信貸收緊拖慢國內消化速度；主要上檔則是持續的 FDI 製造業潮支撐外派與專業租客需求。

HANOI OVERVIEW河內概覽

Hanoi delivered the sharpest price growth among Vietnamese cities in 2025, with primary apartment prices rising +7 to +12% YoY to approximately US$2,865/m² in Q1 2025 and pushing above US$3,100/m² through 2026. The outperformance is driven by a combination of acute supply shortage and the city's emergence as a northern manufacturing administrative hub — Hanoi itself is not the factory location, but it is where Korean, Japanese, and Taiwanese corporates headquartering in the north place their expatriate families.

Supply is the dominant variable. New launches in Hanoi reached a five-year high of over 30,000 units in 2025, with 80% concentrated in three western and southern districts: Hoai Duc, Dong Anh, and Hoang Mai. Grade B stock dominates, reflecting the structural shift away from luxury — developers have retreated from the >US$4,000/m² segment after the 2022 credit crunch exposed weak absorption at those price points. The 80,900 units pipeline from 99 projects entering the market from 2026 onwards represents significant future supply, though timing risk remains material given Vietnam's historical pattern of approval delays.

Rental yields in Hanoi run 5–7% gross — higher than HCMC because rental growth has lagged price growth, creating a price-to-rent compression that favours income-focused buyers. The Samsung and LG industrial corridors in Bac Ninh and Thai Nguyen drive peripheral demand, though the professional-class buyer remains anchored in central and western Hanoi. Hoan Kiem, Ba Dinh, and Tay Ho form the expatriate premium core, while Cau Giay and Nam Tu Liem offer best-value primary market entry at US$2,500–3,000/m². The Hanoi Metro Line 2 (Nhon–Hanoi Station) and Line 3 are under construction with phased openings through 2027.

河內是 2025 年越南漲幅最劇烈的城市，一手公寓價格年增 +7 至 +12%，2025 年第一季均價 US$2,865/m²，2026 年推進到 US$3,100/m² 以上。表現勝過胡志明市，是兩個原因疊加：供給嚴重短缺，加上河內作為北方製造業行政總部的角色——工廠其實不在河內，但韓、日、台企業在北越設總部後，外派家庭都住在河內。

供給是主導變數。2025 年河內新推案量達五年高點超過 30,000 戶，其中 80% 集中在西南三個區：懷德、東英、皇梅。B 級公寓為主流，反映 2022 年信貸危機暴露高價段去化不力後，開發商從 US$4,000/m² 以上的豪宅段撤退。2026 年起將有 80,900 戶、99 個項目陸續進場，供給量相當可觀，但考量越南歷來的核准延誤紀錄，交屋時程風險不小。

河內毛租金收益率 5–7%，比胡志明市高，因為租金漲幅沒跟上房價，租價比被壓縮，反而利於收租型買家。北寧與太原的三星、LG 產業走廊帶動外圍需求，但專業階層買家仍集中在市中心與西區。還劍、巴亭、西湖是外籍高端核心，紙橋、南慈廉則是一手市場最划算的進場點，每平方米 US$2,500–3,000。地鐵 2 號線（仁－河內站）與 3 號線正在施工，預計 2027 年前分階段通車。

OUTLOOK 2026–2028展望 2026–2028

Vietnam's 2026–2028 outlook is defined by three moving parts: (1) the speed of SBV credit normalisation and its transmission to mortgage rates; (2) the pace at which project-approval bottlenecks release new supply into the market; and (3) the trajectory of FDI-driven manufacturing demand, which anchors the expatriate rental base. Unlike Malaysia (structural oversupply) or Japan (supply-constrained demographics), Vietnam's defining feature is the tension between strong underlying demand and administrative/credit frictions that prevent supply and affordability from rebalancing.

Base case (50% probability). SBV maintains its current policy stance with gradual mortgage rate normalisation toward 7.5–8.5% by end-2027. Project approvals continue their slow recovery with Hanoi delivering 28–32K units annually and HCMC 10–14K. FDI stays above US$22B annually. National price growth moderates from 2025's double-digit pace to +5 to +8% in HCMC and +6 to +10% in Hanoi, with growth leadership concentrating in infrastructure-adjacent sub-markets (HCMC Metro L2/L3 corridors, Hanoi L3 corridor). Rental yields compress modestly to 4–6% in HCMC and 5–6.5% in Hanoi.

Bull case (30% probability). Long Thanh Airport opens on schedule in late 2026 with measurable positive commercial impact; SBV eases credit controls on real estate in H2 2027; FDI accelerates to US$27–30B on continued China-diversification and Indo-Pacific supply-chain realignment. Mortgage rates fall toward 7%. National prices rise +10 to +14% with Hanoi outperforming HCMC by approximately 200 bps. Foreign buying volume accelerates +25% YoY as Vietnam's profile rises; some prime Hanoi and HCMC projects hit their 30% cap earlier in the pre-sale cycle. Rental growth re-accelerates as economic momentum drives expat inflows.

Bear case (20% probability). Global risk-off (US recession, China property crisis spillover, or sustained JPY/USD strength) triggers FDI pullback to below US$18B. SBV tightens further to defend the VND, pushing mortgage rates to 10%+. The 80,900-unit Hanoi pipeline delivers into a softening demand environment, and overhang begins to build. Developers with legacy high-priced stock face extended marketing periods. National prices go flat to -3% in HCMC and flat to +2% in Hanoi (still supported by supply scarcity). Rental yields hold or expand as prices soften faster than rents.

Three indicators to monitor quarterly: (1) SBV quarterly credit growth allocation to real estate — the single most important leading indicator of domestic absorption; (2) monthly FDI disbursement data from MPI — direct signal of manufacturing tailwind durability; (3) foreign quota utilisation in major pre-launch projects — the cleanest read on cross-border demand momentum. The underlying thesis remains: Vietnam is APAC's highest-growth frontier market but execution risk is material. Positioning requires active selection of infrastructure-adjacent projects (metro corridors, airport expansion zones), avoiding speculative resort markets (Da Nang excess, Nha Trang softness), and maintaining exit flexibility through Vietnamese-national transfer channels.

越南 2026–2028 年展望由三個變數交織：（1）SBV 信貸正常化速度、以及對房貸利率的傳導；（2）項目審批瓶頸釋出新供給的節奏；（3）FDI 驅動的製造業需求——這是外派租屋基礎的錨點。跟馬來西亞（結構性供過於求）或日本（人口收縮但供給緊）不同，越南的核心特徵是：需求基本面強勁，但行政與信貸摩擦阻止供給與可負擔性重新平衡。

基本情境（機率 50%）。SBV 維持現行政策，2027 年底房貸利率緩步降到 7.5–8.5%。項目審批續漸恢復，河內每年交出 2.8–3.2 萬戶，胡志明市 1–1.4 萬戶。FDI 維持在每年 US$220 億以上。全國漲幅從 2025 年的雙位數收斂到 HCMC +5 至 +8%、河內 +6 至 +10%，漲幅集中在基建沿線（HCMC 2/3 號線走廊、河內 3 號線走廊）。租金收益率 HCMC 輕微收斂到 4–6%、河內 5–6.5%。

樂觀情境（機率 30%）。龍城機場 2026 年底準時開始營運、帶出可測量的商業效益；SBV 在 2027 下半年放鬆房地產信貸管制；FDI 因為中國供應鏈持續分流、加上印太供應鏈重組，加速到 US$270–300 億。房貸利率降到 7%。全國漲幅 +10 至 +14%，河內比胡志明市高約 200 bps。外資買盤加速年增 +25%，部分河內、HCMC 熱門案在預售階段就觸到 30% 外資上限。經濟動能帶動外派流入加速，租金也重新加速。

悲觀情境（機率 20%）。全球風險規避（美國衰退、中國房地產危機外溢、或 JPY/USD 持續強勢）引爆 FDI 回落到 US$180 億以下。SBV 為守匯率進一步緊縮，房貸利率升到 10% 以上。河內 80,900 戶供給線進入轉弱的需求環境，餘屋開始累積。帶著高價遺留庫存的建商銷售週期拉長。HCMC 全國房價持平至 -3%，河內持平至 +2%（仍有供給稀缺撐底）。租金收益率維持或小幅擴張，因為房價修正比租金快。

三個每季要追蹤的指標：（1）SBV 每季對房地產的信貸成長配置——國內消化速度最重要的領先指標；（2）計劃投資部的月度 FDI 到位資料——直接判斷製造業順風的持續性；（3）主要預售案的外資配額使用率——跨境需求動能最乾淨的指標。核心論點維持不變：越南是亞太最高成長的前沿市場，但執行風險不容忽視。布局上要主動挑基建沿線項目（地鐵、機場擴展區），避開投機性度假市場（峴港過剩、芽莊疲軟），並透過轉讓給越南籍買家的管道保留退場彈性。
