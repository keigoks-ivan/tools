Key Risk Factors主要風險因素

Legal & Regulatory法律與監管

Project approval delays average 3-5 years, land use rights complexity, frequent policy changes create uncertainty for developers and foreign buyers. The legal framework for foreign ownership remains evolving and enforcement varies by locality.項目審批延遲平均3-5年，土地使用權複雜，頻繁的政策變動為開發商和外國買家帶來不確定性。外國人持有的法律框架仍在演變，各地執行力度不一。

Transparency透明度

RICS rates Vietnam as "low transparency" market, limited reliable data, informal market practices, title verification challenges for foreigners. Price discovery relies heavily on brokers rather than public transaction records.RICS將越南評為「低透明度」市場，可靠數據有限，非正式市場慣例，外國人面臨產權驗證挑戰。價格發現高度依賴仲介而非公開交易記錄。

Currency匯率風險

VND managed float with 3% annual depreciation trend erodes USD returns, SBV intervention capacity limited by reserves. Foreign investors face repatriation risks and conversion delays during periods of currency stress.越南盾管理浮動匯率制下年均3%的貶值趨勢侵蝕美元回報，越南央行干預能力受外匯儲備限制。外國投資者在貨幣壓力期間面臨資金匯出風險和兌換延遲。

Developer Risk開發商風險

Corporate bond crisis of 2023 exposed overleveraged developers, several major groups restructuring, buyer deposits at risk in delayed projects. Due diligence on developer financial health is critical before committing capital.2023年企業債危機暴露開發商過度槓桿問題，多家大型集團正在重組，延遲項目中買家訂金面臨風險。在投入資金前，對開發商財務狀況進行盡職調查至關重要。
