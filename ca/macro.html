Monetary Cycle × House Prices貨幣週期 × 房價
What to watch: Canada's HPI is extremely rate-sensitive — the 2020–21 zero-rate era pushed prices +50% in two years. The 2022–23 hike cycle reversed ~15% of those gains. BoC's aggressive 2024–25 cuts (−225bp) are now re-igniting demand, but affordability remains stretched at near-record price-to-income multiples.
重點觀察：加拿大HPI對利率極度敏感——2020–21年零利率時代推動房價兩年內上漲50%。2022–23年升息週期使這些漲幅回吐約15%。BoC在2024–25年激進降息225bp，正在重新點燃需求，但可負擔性仍因近歷史高位的房價所得比而面臨壓力。

Real Economy × House Prices實體經濟 × 房價
What to watch: Immigration has been Canada's demand floor — net intake of 500K+/yr (2022–24) absorbs supply even as prices correct. The 2025 government cap on temporary residents (down to 5% of population) is a structural shift that may dampen rental and purchase demand from newcomers.
重點觀察：移民是加拿大需求的底部——2022–24年每年淨移入50萬以上，即使房價修正仍能吸收供給。2025年政府對臨時居民設上限（降至總人口5%）是結構性轉變，可能壓抑新移民的租賃和購屋需求。

Key Cycle Events (2008–2025)關鍵週期事件（2008–2025）
2008–2009 · GFC SHOCK2008–2009 · GFC衝擊
BoC Slashes to 0.25% — HPI Dips BrieflyBoC降至0.25%，HPI短暫下跌
BoC cuts from 4.25% to 0.25% in seven months — fastest easing since 1992. National average price falls ~13% peak-to-trough (2008). Recovery is swift: CREA composite rebounds +20% by end of 2009 as record low rates attract buyers.BoC在七個月內從4.25%降至0.25%——為1992年以來最快寬鬆。全國平均房價從峰值下跌約13%（2008年）。復甦迅速：隨著創紀錄低利率吸引買家，CREA綜合指數在2009年底前反彈約20%。
2010–2016 · SUSTAINED BOOM2010–2016 · 持續榮景
Low Rates + Immigration Drive Decade-Long Bull低利率+移民驅動十年多頭
Post-GFC recovery morphs into a multi-year structural bull. BoC holds at 1% from 2010–17 (longest pause in history). Vancouver and Toronto prices double. Federal CMHC mortgage insurance enables leveraged entry. Immigration adds ~250K/yr to demand.後GFC復甦演變為多年結構性多頭。BoC從2010–17年維持在1%（歷史上最長暫停）。溫哥華和多倫多房價翻倍。聯邦CMHC按揭保險使高槓桿進場成為可能。移民每年增加約25萬需求。
JAN 2018 · OSFI B-20 STRESS TEST2018年1月 · OSFI B-20壓力測試
Stress Test at Contract Rate +2% Squeezes Buyers合約利率+2%壓力測試擠壓買家
OSFI's B-20 guideline requires all uninsured mortgages to qualify at the higher of the contract rate + 2% or 5.25%. Effective buying power cut by ~20%. National HPI corrects −8% in 2018. Vancouver prices fall −10% within 12 months. Toronto pauses then rebounds.OSFI B-20規定所有未保險按揭須按合約利率+2%或5.25%中較高者通過資格測試。有效購買力削減約20%。全國HPI在2018年修正−8%。溫哥華房價在12個月內下跌−10%，多倫多暫停後反彈。
2020–2021 · COVID STIMULUS2020–2021 · COVID刺激
BoC to 0.25% + QE — HPI Surges +40%BoC降至0.25%+QE，HPI暴漲+40%
Emergency rate cut to 0.25% plus C$5bn/week bond purchases. Work-from-home demand shifts buyers toward suburbs and smaller cities. National average soars C$480K → C$680K (+42%) in 18 months. Condo market initially lags, then catches up in late 2021.緊急降息至0.25%加每週50億加元債券購買。在家工作需求將買家轉移至郊區和中小城市。全國均價在18個月內從48萬暴漲至68萬加元（+42%）。公寓市場初期滯後，2021年底追趕上來。
MAR 2022 – JUL 2023 · RATE SHOCK2022年3月–2023年7月 · 升息衝擊
10 Hikes: 0.25% → 5.00% in 16 Months16個月10次升息：0.25% → 5.00%
Fastest tightening cycle in 40 years. Variable-rate mortgage holders face payment shocks. National HPI falls −15% peak-to-trough. Toronto −18%, Vancouver −12%. Activity freezes as buyers wait for clarity. Condo investors face negative cash flow at current rents.40年來最快緊縮週期。浮動利率按揭持有人面臨還款衝擊。全國HPI從峰值下跌−15%，多倫多−18%，溫哥華−12%。買家觀望導致成交量急凍。公寓投資者在當前租金水準面臨負現金流。
2023 · IMMIGRATION SURGE2023年 · 移民潮
900K Net Arrivals — Rental Crisis Deepens淨移入90萬人，租屋危機加深
Canada adds 1.2M population (net +900K) — highest on record. Rental vacancy falls to 1.5% nationally, under 1% in Toronto and Vancouver. Average rent hits C$2,200/mo (+9% YoY). Housing starts fail to keep pace. Political backlash builds against immigration levels.加拿大新增120萬人口（淨+90萬）——創歷史紀錄。全國租屋空置率降至1.5%，多倫多和溫哥華低於1%。平均租金達2,200加元/月（YoY+9%）。住宅開工未能跟上需求，政界對移民規模的反彈聲浪加劇。
JUN 2024 · BoC LEADS G7 IN CUTS2024年6月 · BoC領先G7降息
First G7 Central Bank to Cut — Signals New CycleG7首家降息央行，宣告新週期
BoC cuts June 2024 — ahead of Fed and ECB. By April 2025, BoC has cut 225bp to 2.75%. Sales volumes rise but prices remain flat to modestly up (national +1–3%). Market bifurcates: single-family homes recover while condo inventory builds to decade highs in Toronto.BoC於2024年6月率先降息，早於Fed和ECB。至2025年4月，BoC已降息225bp至2.75%。銷售量上升，但房價大致持平略升（全國+1–3%）。市場分化：獨立屋復甦，多倫多公寓庫存累積至十年高點。
2025 · IMMIGRATION CAP + TARIFF RISK2025年 · 移民上限+關稅風險
Ottawa Caps Temporary Residents; US Tariffs Cloud Outlook聯邦限制臨時居民；美國關稅籠罩前景
Government targets temporary residents at 5% of population (from 7.3%), reducing demand from international students and workers. US tariff threat (25% on Canadian goods) adds recession risk — BoC has room to cut further. Structural housing deficit (3.5M units by 2030 per CMHC) underpins long-term demand.政府目標將臨時居民降至人口的5%（從7.3%），減少國際學生和工人的需求。美國關稅威脅（加拿大商品加徵25%）增加衰退風險——BoC有空間進一步降息。結構性住房缺口（CMHC預計2030年缺350萬套）支撐長期需求。
