Key Risk Factors主要風險因素

Household Debt家庭負債

At 101% of GDP, Canada's household debt is among the highest in the G7 and globally. The combination of high mortgage balances, variable-rate exposure (approximately 30% of outstanding mortgages), and elevated housing costs creates systemic vulnerability. A significant portion of mortgages originated during 2020–2021 at ultra-low rates face renewal shock at substantially higher rates through 2026.家庭負債佔GDP 101%，為G7及全球最高之一。高房貸餘額、浮動利率曝險（約佔未償還房貸30%）和高昂住房成本的組合造成系統性脆弱性。2020至2021年超低利率期間發放的大量房貸在2026年前面臨大幅提高利率的續約衝擊。

Foreign Buyer Ban外國買家禁令

Canada's Prohibition on the Purchase of Residential Property by Non-Canadians Act, extended to January 2027, removes a significant source of demand particularly in Vancouver and Toronto. While exemptions exist for permanent residents and certain property types, the ban signals a structural policy shift that may deter international capital flows even after expiry.加拿大《禁止非加拿大人購買住宅物業法》延長至2027年1月，移除了溫哥華和多倫多的重要需求來源。雖然永久居民和某些物業類型有豁免，但該禁令標誌著結構性政策轉向，即使到期後也可能阻礙國際資本流入。

Condo Oversupply公寓供給過剩

Toronto's condo market faces rising investor exits as pre-construction units close at prices below original purchase levels. Negative cash flow from rising rates has pushed many investor-owners to list, increasing supply at a time when demand is constrained by the stress test qualifying rate of 5.25%. Completion volumes in 2025–2026 are at record highs, adding further pressure.多倫多公寓市場面臨投資者退出增加，預售單位以低於原始購買價的價格交割。升息帶來的負現金流促使許多投資者業主掛牌出售，在需求受5.25%壓力測試合格利率約束的時期增加供給。2025至2026年竣工量創歷史新高，進一步加大壓力。

Immigration Dependency移民依賴

Canada's housing demand has been heavily driven by record immigration levels exceeding 1 million per year. Political pressure to reduce immigration targets could significantly impact demand fundamentals, particularly in the rental market. Any substantial reduction in immigration intake would remove a key price support, especially in Toronto and Vancouver where newcomer settlement is concentrated.加拿大住房需求受到每年超過100萬人的創紀錄移民水平大力推動。減少移民目標的政治壓力可能顯著影響需求基本面，尤其是租賃市場。任何大幅削減移民配額都將移除關鍵價格支撐，特別是在新移民聚居的多倫多和溫哥華。
