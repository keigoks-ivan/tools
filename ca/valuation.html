Valuation Framework估值框架

Price-to-Income房價所得比

Vancouver's PTI of 12.5x is the worst in Canada and among the highest in the G7. Toronto at 10.5x is also severely unaffordable. Edmonton (4.5x) and Calgary (5.0x) remain the most accessible markets, near the national historical average of 5.1x. The prairie cities offer a stark contrast to the coastal unaffordability crisis.溫哥華房價所得比12.5倍為加拿大最差，也是G7中最高之一。多倫多10.5倍同樣嚴重不可負擔。艾德蒙頓（4.5倍）和卡加利（5.0倍）仍是最可負擔市場，接近全國歷史平均5.1倍。草原城市與沿海負擔能力危機形成鮮明對比。

Price-to-Rent房價租金比

Vancouver's P/R of 32x places it alongside London (31x) among the most overvalued G7 cities on a rental income basis. Toronto at 26x is comparable to Berlin. Both Canadian markets offer yields that barely cover borrowing costs at current BOC rates, making pure rental income strategies challenging without capital appreciation assumptions.溫哥華租金比32倍與倫敦（31倍）並列G7中租金收入角度最被高估的城市。多倫多26倍與柏林相當。兩個加拿大市場的收益率在當前央行利率下勉強覆蓋借貸成本，純租金收入策略在不假設資本增值的情況下充滿挑戰。

Yield Spread利差分析

The +0.55% spread is a significant improvement from the -1.0% negative carry during the 2023 rate peak, but remains well below the +2.0% spread that characterized the pre-pandemic era. Further BOC cuts toward 2.5% would restore meaningful positive carry. Variable-rate mortgage holders are the primary beneficiaries of the easing cycle.+0.55%的利差相較2023年利率高峰時的-1.0%負利差有顯著改善，但仍遠低於疫情前+2.0%的利差水平。BOC進一步降息至2.5%將恢復有意義的正利差。浮動利率房貸持有者是降息週期的主要受益者。
