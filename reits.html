P/NAV Deep DiveP/NAV 深度解讀
  • <0.7x — Deep discount. Either deep value or structural distress. Check: is occupancy falling? Is gearing rising? If yes = value trap. If no = opportunity. Examples: Vonovia 0.55x, Unibail 0.50x.<0.7倍 — 深度折價。要嘛是深度價值，要嘛是結構性困境。確認：出租率在降嗎？槓桿在升嗎？是=價值陷阱。否=機會。例：Vonovia 0.55倍、Unibail 0.50倍。
  • 0.7-0.95x — Moderate discount. Most common for office and diversified REITs in rate-hike cycle. Usually reverts to NAV when rates stabilize.0.7-0.95倍 — 適度折價。升息週期中辦公和多元化REIT最常見。利率穩定後通常回歸NAV。
  • 0.95-1.1x — Near NAV. Fair value. The market trusts the property valuation.0.95-1.1倍 — 接近NAV。公允價值。市場信任資產估值。
  • 1.1-1.5x — Growth premium. Market expects acquisition pipeline or rental growth to lift NAV. Justified for logistics/DC sector.1.1-1.5倍 — 成長溢價。市場預期收購管道或租金成長將提升NAV。物流/資料中心可合理。
  • >1.5x — Extreme premium. Only Goodman (2.8x) and Equinix (2.1x) sustain this. Very risky if growth disappoints.>1.5倍 — 極端溢價。僅Goodman（2.8倍）和Equinix（2.1倍）能持續。成長不如預期時風險極高。
Yield vs Bond Spread Strategy殖利率 vs 公債利差策略
  • Spread >+2% — Attractive. REIT pays meaningfully more than risk-free bonds. Sweet spot for income investors. J-REITs and SG-REITs dominate this zone.利差>+2% — 有吸引力。REIT派息明顯高於無風險公債。收入型投資者的甜蜜點。日本REIT和新加坡REIT主導此區間。
  • Spread 0 to +2% — Fair compensation for real estate risk. Acceptable if growth catalysts present.利差0至+2% — 對房地產風險的合理補償。若有成長催化劑則可接受。
  • Spread negative — Bonds beat REIT on income. You must believe in capital appreciation to justify holding. Most US REITs are here because UST 10yr = 4.25%.利差為負 — 公債收入勝過REIT。必須相信資本增值才合理。多數美國REIT在此，因美債10年期=4.25%。
  • Key: When 10yr bond yields fall, REIT spreads widen automatically = price appreciation. This is why REITs rally hard in rate-cut cycles.關鍵：10年期公債殖利率下降時，REIT利差自動擴大=價格上漲。這是降息週期中REIT大漲的原因。
Gearing & Interest Cover槓桿率與利息保障
  • <25% gearing — Conservative. Goodman at 10% is the gold standard. Lots of firepower for acquisitions.<25%槓桿 — 保守。Goodman 10%是黃金標準。有充足收購火力。
  • 25-40% — Normal range. Most healthy REITs operate here. Comfortable debt servicing.25-40% — 正常區間。多數健康REIT在此運作。債務償付舒適。
  • 40-50% — Elevated. Each 25bp rate hike visibly impacts DPU. Check: % of debt on fixed vs floating rates. High fixed = protected.40-50% — 偏高。每升25bp明顯衝擊DPU。檢查：固定vs浮動利率債務比例。高固定=受保護。
  • Interest cover ratio — EBITDA ÷ interest expense. >4x = comfortable. 3-4x = monitor. <3x = refinancing risk. <2x = DPU cut imminent.利息保障倍數 — EBITDA ÷ 利息支出。>4倍=舒適。3-4倍=需監測。<3倍=再融資風險。<2倍=DPU削減在即。
  • SG rule: MAS caps REIT gearing at 50%. Exceeding 45% requires higher interest cover ratio disclosure.新加坡規則：MAS將REIT槓桿上限設在50%。超過45%需更高的利息保障揭露。

DPU Analysis FrameworkDPU分析框架
  • DPU = Distribution Per Unit — the actual cash paid to you. This is the REIT equivalent of EPS. Track quarterly.DPU = 每單位配息 — 實際付給你的現金。相當於REIT版的EPS。每季追蹤。
  • DPU growth drivers: rental reversion (rent renewal at higher rate) + acquisitions + organic occupancy improvement + cost reduction.DPU成長動力：租金調升（續約時提高租金）+收購+有機出租率改善+成本削減。
  • DPU threats: vacancies, negative rental reversion, rising interest costs, dilutive equity raises, currency depreciation (for cross-border portfolios).DPU威脅：空置、負向租金調整、利息成本上升、稀釋性增資、匯率貶值（跨境組合）。
  • Golden rule: If DPU has grown every year for 5+ years, the manager is doing their job. If DPU has fallen 2+ consecutive periods, investigate immediately.黃金法則：若DPU連續5年以上每年成長，管理層在做事。若DPU連續2期以上下滑，立即調查。
WALE & OccupancyWALE與出租率
  • WALE (Weighted Average Lease Expiry) — how many years until all leases need renewing. >5yr = very safe. 3-5yr = OK. <3yr = renewal risk.WALE（加權平均租約到期年）— 所有租約還有多久要續約。>5年=非常安全。3-5年=尚可。<3年=續約風險。
  • Occupancy >95% = healthy. 90-95% = normal for diversified. <90% = structural problem (sector or location).出租率>95%=健康。90-95%=多元化型正常。<90%=結構性問題（產業或地點）。
  • Tenant concentration: If top tenant = 20%+ of income, that one lease renewal defines DPU. Prefer top 10 tenants <50%.租客集中度：若最大租客佔收入20%+，那一份續約就決定了DPU。偏好前10大租客佔<50%。
  • Rental reversion: Positive = organic growth. Negative = the REIT is losing pricing power. Track this quarterly.租金調升率：正值=有機成長。負值=REIT正在失去定價能力。每季追蹤。
Red Flags & Warning Signs紅旗與警訊
  • 🔴 DPU cut — Typically triggers 10-20% price drop. Exit immediately unless you understand the reason and it is temporary.🔴 DPU削減 — 通常觸發10-20%暴跌。除非你理解原因且是暫時性的，否則立即退出。
  • 🔴 Gearing >45% + floating rate debt >50% = double exposure to rate hikes. Interest costs can double in 12 months.🔴 槓桿>45%+浮動利率債務>50%=對升息雙重曝險。利息成本可在12個月內翻倍。
  • 🔴 Payout >100% = distributing more than earned. Funded by debt or capital returns. Unsustainable beyond 2-3 quarters.🔴 配息率>100%=分配超過賺取。靠債務或資本回饋支撐。超過2-3季不可持續。
  • 🔴 Negative spread + gearing >40% + YoY negative = triple warning signal. All three simultaneously = sell.🔴 負利差+槓桿>40%+YoY負數=三重警訊。三者同時發生=賣出。
  • 🔴 Manager equity raise at deep discount (>10% below market) = dilution. Your unit value drops immediately. Check if acquisition is truly accretive.🔴 管理層大幅折價增資（低於市價>10%）=稀釋。你的單位價值立即下降。確認收購是否真正增值。

③ Total Return Calculation③ 總報酬計算
REIT Total Return =
Capital gain/loss (buy vs sell price)
+ Cumulative dividends received
− Withholding tax on dividends
− Brokerage & platform fees
± FX gain/loss (cross-border)

Compare this to: simply holding the 10yr bond of the same country for the same period. If the bond wins, the REIT wasn't worth the risk. REIT總報酬 =
資本利得/損失（買入 vs 賣出價）
+ 累計收到的股息
− 股息預扣稅
− 券商與平台手續費
± 匯率損益（跨境投資）

對比：同期間持有同國10年期公債。如果公債贏了，REIT就不值得承擔那個風險。
