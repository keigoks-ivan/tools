SUMMARY總結

Yield收益率

4.69%
National gross rental yield remains above the RBA cash rate, offering a positive carry of +0.84%. The spread has compressed since the Feb 2026 rate cut but remains in positive territory.全國毛租金收益率仍高於RBA基準利率，提供+0.84%正向現金流。自2026年2月降息後利差收窄，但仍維持正值。

Supply供給

Deficit短缺
Housing starts remain well below the 300K annual target. Construction costs +25-29% since 2020 and labour shortages continue to constrain the pipeline. Structural undersupply supports prices.住宅開工量遠低於年度30萬套目標。自2020年以來營建成本上漲25-29%，勞動力短缺持續制約在建供給。結構性供不應求支撐房價。

Affordability負擔能力

Stretched緊繃
Sydney requires 85 years of median household savings to purchase. National price-to-income at 8.2x remains severely unaffordable by global standards. Policy interventions expanding but insufficient.雪梨需要85年的家庭中位數儲蓄才能購屋。全國房價所得比8.2倍，以全球標準衡量屬嚴重不可負擔。政策干預擴大但仍不足。
