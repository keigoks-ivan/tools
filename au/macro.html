Monetary Cycle × House Prices貨幣週期 × 房價
What to watch: Australia's RBA cut rates to a record-low 0.10% in Nov 2020, triggering the fastest property price surge in decades — national HPI rose ~28% in under two years. The subsequent 425bp hike cycle (May 2022 – Nov 2023) was the fastest since 1994 yet only produced a ~9% peak-to-trough correction, highlighting extreme supply inelasticity and record immigration.
重點觀察：RBA於2020年11月將利率降至歷史低點0.10%，觸發數十年來最快的房價飆升——全國HPI在不到兩年內上漲約28%。隨後425bp的升息週期（2022年5月至2023年11月）為1994年以來最快，但僅造成約9%的高峰至低谷修正，凸顯了極度的供給剛性以及創紀錄的移民潮。

Real Economy × House Prices實體經濟 × 房價
What to watch: The 2018 Banking Royal Commission forced mortgage lending standards sharply higher — credit growth cratered from ~7% to near 2%, directly causing the 2018–19 house price correction of ~10%. Immigration (net overseas arrivals, dashed) is now running at record ~600k/yr, structurally overwhelming new housing supply and preventing sustained price falls.
重點觀察：2018年銀行皇家委員會迫使房貸標準大幅提升——信貸成長從約7%跌至近2%，直接造成2018–19年房價約10%的修正。目前移民人數（淨海外抵達，虛線）年達創紀錄約60萬人，從結構上壓倒新房供給，阻止了持續性房價下跌。

2008–2009 · GFC SHOCK2008–2009 · GFC衝擊
RBA Cuts 425bp — Australia Avoids RecessionRBA降息425bp，澳洲躲過衰退
RBA slashes cash rate from 7.25% to 3.00% in 7 months — fastest easing since 1990. Federal government's A$42bn stimulus package. Australia avoids technical recession (GDP −0.5% Q4 2008 but rebounds in 2009). HPI falls only ~3% before recovering. First Home Owner Boost doubling grant to A$21k supercharges demand.RBA在7個月內將現金利率從7.25%急降至3.00%，為1990年以來最快降息。聯邦政府推出420億澳元刺激計劃，澳洲避免技術性衰退。HPI僅跌約3%即反彈，首次置業補助金提升至2.1萬澳元大力刺激需求。
2010–2017 · POST-GFC BOOM2010–2017 · 後GFC榮景
Sydney & Melbourne Led +80% HPI surge悉尼墨爾本領漲，HPI累升80%
China-driven mining boom sustains employment and income growth. RBA holds rates low (1.50% by Aug 2016). Investor lending surges, with interest-only loans peaking at 40% of new mortgages in 2015. APRA imposes 10% investor loan growth cap in Dec 2014 — slowing but not stopping price gains. Sydney peaks +75% from 2012 trough.中國帶動礦業繁榮，支撐就業與收入增長。RBA維持低利率（2016年8月降至1.50%）。投資者貸款激增，只付利息貸款於2015年達新房貸40%高峰。APRA於2014年12月實施投資者貸款10%增長上限，放緩但未能阻止房價上漲。悉尼從2012低點累漲75%。
2017–2019 · ROYAL COMMISSION2017–2019 · 皇家委員會
Banking Royal Commission Triggers Credit Crunch銀行皇家委員會引發信貸緊縮
Sep 2017: APRA 30% cap on interest-only lending. Apr 2018: Banking Royal Commission interim report exposes systemic misconduct. Banks sharply tighten lending standards. Credit growth craters from 6.5% to 2.5%. Sydney HPI falls −15%, Melbourne −10% (2017 peak to 2019 trough). APRA removes IO lending cap in Dec 2018 after damage done.2017年9月：APRA限制只付利息貸款佔比30%上限。2018年4月：銀行皇家委員會中期報告揭露系統性失當行為，銀行大幅收緊放貸標準，信貸成長從6.5%跌至2.5%。悉尼HPI從2017高點跌15%，墨爾本跌10%。2018年12月APRA取消IO上限，但損害已成。
MAR 2020 · COVID EMERGENCY2020年3月 · COVID緊急應對
RBA to Historic Low 0.10%; GDP −1.9%RBA降至歷史低點0.10%；GDP衰退1.9%
RBA cuts to 0.10% in Nov 2020 — lowest ever. A$319bn QE bond purchase program launched (yield curve control). GDP contracts −1.9% (2020 annual). HPI falls −3% but bottoms quickly. Immigration borders closed — rental vacancy plunges, setting up structural shortage. JobKeeper wage subsidy prevents mass unemployment.RBA於2020年11月降至0.10%歷史低點，啟動3190億澳元QE購債計劃（殖利率曲線控制）。GDP年衰退1.9%，HPI跌3%後迅速見底。移民邊境關閉導致租屋空置率暴跌，埋下結構性短缺。政府支薪補貼防止大規模失業。
2021 · COVID PRICE SURGE2021年 · COVID後房價飆升
National HPI +22% — Fastest Since 1980s全國HPI+22%，為1980年代以來最快
Ultra-low rates, A$25k HomeBuilder grant, stamp duty waivers and pandemic-driven space demand combine for the strongest house price surge in 40+ years. Every capital city records double-digit annual gains. Regional areas surge even faster as remote work enables migration away from CBDs. Credit growth rebounds to 7%+.超低利率、2.5萬澳元HomeBuilder補助、印花稅豁免及疫情帶動的居住空間需求，共同造就40年來最強的房價飆升。每個首府城市均錄得雙位數年漲幅，偏遠地區因遠端工作趨勢漲速更快。信貸成長反彈至7%以上。
MAY 2022 · RATE HIKE SHOCK2022年5月 · 升息衝擊
13 Hikes: 0.10% → 4.35% in 18 Months18個月13次升息：0.10%→4.35%
RBA begins hike cycle May 2022 after insisting rates won't rise until 2024. 13 consecutive hikes by Nov 2023 — 425bp in 18 months, fastest since 1994. National HPI falls −9% peak-to-trough. Sydney −14%, Melbourne −10%. Mortgage stress spikes: avg new borrower pays 43% of income on mortgage. RBA forward guidance credibility damaged.RBA於2022年5月啟動升息週期，此前曾堅稱2024年前不會升息。2023年11月前共升息13次，18個月累計425bp，為1994年以來最快。全國HPI峰谷修正9%，悉尼跌14%，墨爾本跌10%。房貸壓力激增：平均新借款人需用43%收入還款。RBA前瞻指引公信力受損。
2023–2024 · REBOUND ON RECORD MIGRATION2023–2024 · 創紀錄移民帶動反彈
Net Overseas Migration 620k — HPI Rebounds淨海外移民62萬人，HPI反彈
Despite RBA holding at 4.35%, national HPI rebounds +8.5% in 2023 and +5.6% in 2024 — driven by record 620,000 net overseas arrivals (2022–23) creating acute rental and purchase demand. National vacancy rate drops to 1%. Rental CPI running at 7%+ YoY. Government housing supply target (1.2M homes by 2029) widely seen as unachievable.儘管RBA維持4.35%，全國HPI於2023年反彈8.5%、2024年再漲5.6%——創紀錄的62萬淨海外移民（2022–23年）製造急迫的租房與購房需求。全國空置率跌至1%，租金CPI以每年7%以上速度上漲。政府到2029年供應120萬套住宅目標被廣泛認為無法達成。
FEB 2025 · RBA EASING BEGINS2025年2月 · RBA降息開始
First Cut Since 2020; CPI Back in Band2020年以來首次降息；CPI回落目標帶
RBA cuts 25bp to 4.10% in Feb 2025 — first rate cut since Nov 2020. CPI returns to 2–3% target band (2.4% Q1 2025). Market pricing 2–3 further cuts in 2025. Immigration moderating from record highs (still ~450k net). Labour market softening (unemployment 4.1%). HPI growth moderating but no significant correction expected given supply deficit.RBA於2025年2月降息25bp至4.10%，為2020年11月以來首次降息。CPI回落至2–3%目標帶（2025年Q1為2.4%）。市場預期2025年再降息2–3次。移民從歷史高點趨緩（仍約45萬淨移民）。就業市場軟化（失業率4.1%）。受供給缺口支撐，HPI漲速趨緩但預計不會大幅修正。
