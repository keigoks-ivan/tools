SUPPLY: STRUCTURAL BOTTLENECKS供給面：系統性瓶頸

NSW needs 75,400 new homes per year to meet the National Housing Accord target of 377,000 by 2029. Reality: only 29,900 expected in 2025-26 — a 60% shortfall. National forecast gap: 262,000 homes by 2029. Building approvals collapsed 7.2% in January 2026; private sector apartments down 24.5%. Key bottlenecks: high financing costs (4.10% cash rate), chronic skilled labour shortage, and Sydney Water infrastructure constraints blocking TOD developments.NSW需每年新建75,400套才能完成《國家住房協議》至2029年的37.7萬套目標。現實：2025-26年預計僅完工29,900套——缺口高達60%。全國預測缺口：至2029年累計26.2萬套。2026年1月建築批准量暴跌7.2%，私人公寓更下滑24.5%。關鍵瓶頸：融資成本高昂（現金利率4.10%）、熟練勞工嚴重短缺，以及雪梨水務公司基礎設施限制阻礙TOD開發。

RENTAL MARKET CRISIS租賃市場危機

Sydney vacancy rate: 1.5% in Jan 2026, tightening to 1.3% by March. Equilibrium is 2.5-3.0%. Median house rent: A$800/week (+3.9% YoY); Units: A$750/week (+7.1% YoY). Domain forecasts house rents hitting A$815/week in 2026. But rent growth is slowing — not from new supply, but because tenants have hit their absolute affordability ceiling. NSW rental reforms (no-grounds eviction ban, annual rent cap) risk triggering investor exit and permanent reduction in rental supply.雪梨空置率：2026年1月1.5%，3月進一步緊縮至1.3%。均衡水位為2.5-3.0%。獨棟中位租金A$800/週（年增3.9%）；公寓A$750/週（年增7.1%）。Domain預測2026年獨棟週租將達A$815。但租金增速放緩——並非因為新供給出現，而是租客負擔能力已觸頂。NSW租賃改革（禁止無理由驅逐、年度租金上調限制）存在引發投資者撤出並永久削減租賃供給的風險。

POLICY IMPACT & FIRST HOME BUYERS政策干預與首購族效應

From 1 Oct 2025: First Home Guarantee expanded to unlimited places, no income caps, Sydney price cap raised from A$900K to A$1.5M. NSW stamp duty exemption for homes under A$800K. These policies are compressing demand into the A$800K–$1.5M band, creating fierce competition and forming a price floor at the cap ceiling. Bottom quartile prices rose 2.5% in Q1 2026 while premium fell 2.0% — textbook policy-driven segmentation.2025年10月1日起：首購族擔保計畫取消名額限制、取消收入上限，雪梨價格上限從90萬提升至150萬澳元。NSW對80萬以下住宅提供全額印花稅豁免。這些政策將需求壓縮至80至150萬澳元的價格帶，在政策上限附近製造激烈競爭並形成支撐。2026年Q1低價位段漲2.5%，高端跌2.0%——教科書式的政策驅動市場分化。

SUMMARY摘要與結論

Fragile Equilibrium脆弱的僵局

Auction clearance at 55%, vendors holding firm, buyers priced out. Market locked in a standoff until rate cuts emerge.清盤率55%，賣方拒絕降價，買方無力追高。市場陷入僵局，等待降息解套。

Supply Structural Failure供給系統性失靈

NSW will miss the Accord target by ~180,000 units by 2029. Inelastic supply is the price floor — markets don't crash when there's nothing to sell.NSW至2029年供給缺口將達約18萬套。非彈性供給是房價底部——沒有房子可賣時市場不會崩盤。

Rental Crisis Structural租賃危機結構性

Vacancy at 1.5%, rents at absolute affordability ceiling. NSW rental reforms risk investor exit, which would make the crisis worse.空置率1.5%，租金觸及絕對負擔極限。NSW租賃改革存在引發投資者撤出的風險，這反而會惡化危機。

Affordable Segment Opportunity低價位段機會

Policy and affordability constraints are funnelling demand into sub-$1.5M units and western Sydney. This segment has structural support.政策與負擔能力限制正將需求導向150萬以下公寓與西雪梨。此板塊具備結構性支撐。

Watch Triggers關鍵觸發因素

Upside: rate cuts + supply breakthrough. Downside: unemployment spike + second inflation wave. Neither is imminent, but both are non-zero.上行觸發：降息+供給突破。下行觸發：失業率飆升+通膨二次爆發。兩者均未迫在眉睫，但概率非零。
