POPULATION & MIGRATION ENGINE人口爆炸與移民引擎

Queensland's population grew 1.8% in 2025 — well above the 1.6% national average, adding 98,636 people in a single year. Migration drives 79% of growth (vs 58% in the 1980s). NZ migration to Queensland surged 78.4% in two years. Brisbane receives the lion's share of interstate wealthy migrants selling high-priced Sydney/Melbourne homes for cash-rich entry.昆士蘭2025年人口增長1.8%，遠超全國均值1.6%，單年增加98,636人。移民貢獻增長的79%（1980年代僅58%）。紐西蘭移民兩年內激增78.4%。布里斯本吸引大批持有南部高價房屋套現現金的跨州富裕移民。

SUPPLY: SYSTEM FAILURE供給端：系統性崩潰

Queensland needs 49,000 new homes/year under the National Housing Accord. Actual completions: ~34,000. January 2026 approvals trend: 3,555 — running 13% below target annually. Construction cost of a Brisbane apartment surged 19% in 2024 alone to A$690,045. BRISBANE MARKET DYNAMICS布里斯本市場動態

Brisbane has crossed the A$1M median house price threshold — up over 50% since COVID. The "Blanket Price Phenomenon": no decent suburb within 20km of CBD offers entry below A$1.1M. First Home Guarantee (price cap raised to A$1M) and QLD stamp duty exemption create artificial price floors.布里斯本獨棟中位價正式突破百萬澳幣，自COVID以來上漲逾50%。「統一底價現象」：CBD方圓20公里內任何「體面」郊區入門價均不低於A$110萬。首購擔保計畫和昆士蘭印花稅減免在供給真空中製造人為的價格地板。
