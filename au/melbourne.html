SUMMARY總結

Weakest Capital City最弱首府城市

Melbourne's +2% YoY growth lags all other major capitals. Land tax reforms and oversupply in CBD apartments are creating investor hesitation.墨爾本年增+2%落後所有主要首府城市。土地稅改革與CBD公寓供過於求造成投資者觀望。

Outer Growth Story外圍成長故事

Melton and Werribee are posting 5%+ growth, outperforming inner suburbs. New infrastructure and affordability drive demand westward.梅爾頓和韋里比錄得5%以上成長，表現優於內城區。新基建與可負擔性推動需求西移。

Land Tax Headwind土地稅逆風

Victoria's land tax surcharge on investment properties is prompting some investors to divest. This creates buying opportunities for long-term holders.維多利亞州對投資物業的土地稅附加費促使部分投資者出售。為長期持有者創造買入機會。

Population Tailwind人口順風

At 5.3M and growing, Melbourne is on track to overtake Sydney as Australia's largest city. Long-term demand fundamentals remain strong.人口530萬且持續增長，墨爾本有望超越雪梨成為澳洲最大城市。長期需求基本面依然強勁。
