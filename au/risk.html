RISK FACTORS風險因素

RBA PolicyRBA政策

HIGH高

After holding at 4.35% for over two years, the RBA cut to 3.85% in Feb 2026. Further cuts depend on inflation trajectory. Any reversal would severely impact mortgage holders on variable rates.在維持4.35%超過兩年後，RBA於2026年2月降息至3.85%。後續降息取決於通膨走勢。任何逆轉將嚴重衝擊浮動利率房貸持有者。

Affordability Crisis負擔能力危機

HIGH高

Worst affordability since 1995. Sydney requires 85 years of median savings. National price-to-income at 8.2x. Social and political pressure mounting for intervention.自1995年以來最差的負擔能力。雪梨需85年中位數儲蓄。全國房價所得比8.2倍。社會與政治壓力持續增加。

Construction Deficit建設缺口

MEDIUM中

Housing starts ~158K pa vs 300K target. Construction costs +25-29% since 2020. Labour shortages and material costs constrain the pipeline. This supports prices but worsens the housing crisis.住宅開工約15.8萬套/年，遠低於30萬套目標。營建成本自2020年上漲25-29%。勞動力短缺與材料成本制約在建供給，支撐房價但加劇住房危機。

Climate Risk氣候風險

MEDIUM中

Increasing frequency of floods, bushfires, and extreme weather. Insurance costs rising sharply in exposed regions. Potential repricing of coastal and flood-prone properties.洪水、叢林火災和極端天氣頻率增加。高風險地區保險成本急劇上升。沿海和易洪地區房產面臨重新定價風險。
