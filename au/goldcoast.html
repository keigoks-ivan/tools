POPULATION & LIFESTYLE MIGRATION人口與生活方式移民

Gold Coast added 15,300 residents in 2024 alone. It's no longer a "holiday city" — it's a permanently settled high-value lifestyle hub attracting wealth from Sydney and Melbourne. NZ migrants concentrate here: >33% of Australia's NZ-born population lives in Gold Coast/Brisbane.黃金海岸2024年單年新增居民15,300人。已不再是「度假城市」——而是永久定居的高端生活方式樞紐，從雪梨和墨爾本吸引財富湧入。紐西蘭移民高度集中：逾三分之一的在澳紐西蘭人居住在黃金海岸/布里斯本。
