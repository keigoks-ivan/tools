PR status = no foreign-buyer surcharge in any Australian state — a major cost advantagePR 身分 = 澳洲每一州都免外國買家附加稅 — 重大成本優勢 Foreign buyers face stamp duty surcharges ranging from 7% (QLD) to 8% (NSW, VIC).
