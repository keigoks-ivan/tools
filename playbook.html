YIELD TRAP收益率陷阱 🇹🇷 Istanbul · 2018–2026
TRY 5 → 40 vs USD over 8 years (−87% in USD). Property nominal +200%, but USD-denominated investor lost −60% of real capital. Real-terms 2026 YoY: −3.4% despite +27.8% nominal. 8 年內里拉對美元從 5 跌到 40（美元計-87%）。房產名目漲 200%，但美元投資者實質虧 -60%。2026 名目+27.8%，實質 -3.4%。
Lesson: A 7% yield in a currency falling 30%/year nets you −23%. 教訓：年貶 30% 的貨幣中，7% 收益率實際淨值是 -23%。
OVERSUPPLY TRAP供給過剩陷阱 🇵🇭 Manila · 2018–2026
Pre-2018 Chinese POGO demand drove pre-sale boom. POGO ban 2024 + Chinese capital flight = 30,000 unsold condos, 8-year absorption, vacancy 26%. BSP RPPI +1.6% YoY 2026 = lowest in 6 years. Many off-plan buyers cannot find tenants at advertised yields. 2018 前中國 POGO 帶動預售熱潮。2024 POGO 禁令 + 中資撤離 = 3 萬未售公寓，8 年消化，空置 26%。BSP RPPI 2026 +1.6%，6 年新低。許多預售買家找不到租客。
Lesson: A single regulatory event (POGO ban) collapsed an entire pre-sale market overnight. 教訓：單一政策事件（POGO 禁令）一夜壓垮整個預售市場。
POLICY REVERSAL政策反轉 🇸🇬 Singapore · 27 Apr 2023
Foreign buyer ABSD doubled overnight: 30% → 60%. A S$2M condo's stamp duty jumped from S$600K to S$1.2M with zero notice. Foreign buyer transactions collapsed −61% the following quarter. Buyers in mid-purchase had to walk away from deposits. 外國買家 ABSD 一夜翻倍：30% → 60%。S$200 萬公寓印花稅從 S$60 萬跳到 S$120 萬，零預警。下一季外國買家成交 -61%。交易進行中的買家被迫棄訂。
Lesson: Tax tables are not contracts. Always price in 5–15% policy headroom. 教訓：稅表不是合約。預留 5–15% 政策變動空間。
SCARCITY WINS稀缺者勝 🇦🇺 Sydney · 2016–2026
Carry has been negative the whole decade (yield 3.0% − mortgage 5.5% = −2.5%). Yet 10yr cumulative gain +65%. Why: SQM vacancy 1.1%, geographic constraint, near-zero high-rise approvals in established suburbs. Negative carry investors covered the gap from salary; capital appreciation paid them back. 整整 10 年負利差（收益率 3.0% - 房貸 5.5% = -2.5%）。但 10 年累計 +65%。原因：SQM 空置 1.1%、地理限制、舊市區幾乎不核准高樓。負利差投資人用薪水補缺口，最後資本增值還給他們。
Lesson: When supply cannot grow, carry math stops being the answer. 教訓：當供給無法增長，利差計算不再是答案。

Currency & FX Risk匯率與外匯風險
Rule of thumb: A 10% depreciation in property currency wipes out ~2 years of rental income. Your real return = property return + FX return. 經驗法則：當地貨幣貶值10%等於抹去約2年租金收入。你的實際報酬 = 房產報酬 + 匯率報酬。
▲ Strong currency markets (CHF, SGD, USD): FX tailwind for foreign owners. Currency acts as a bonus return layer.強勢貨幣市場（瑞郎、新幣、美元）：匯率為外國業主帶來順風。貨幣本身是額外報酬層。
▼ Weak/volatile currencies (TRY, IDR, PHP, MYR): High yields can be entirely offset by depreciation. Istanbul 25% YoY growth means nothing if lira drops 30%.弱勢/波動貨幣（土耳其里拉、印尼盾、披索、馬幣）：高收益率可能完全被貶值抵消。伊斯坦堡年增25%在里拉跌30%後毫無意義。
▶ Hedging options: USD-pegged markets (Dubai AED, HK HKD) eliminate FX risk vs USD. JPY weakness has been a structural entry discount for Asian buyers since 2022.對沖選項：掛鉤美元市場（杜拜迪拉姆、港幣）消除對美元匯率風險。日圓弱勢自2022年起為亞洲買家提供結構性進場折價。
Vacancy & Tenant Risk空租與租客風險
Actual yield = gross yield × occupancy rate. A 6% yield at 80% occupancy = 4.8%. 實際收益率 = 毛收益率 × 出租率。6%收益率在80%出租率下 = 實際4.8%。

Total Return Formula總報酬公式
True Return =
Capital gain (local currency)
+ Cumulative net rental income
− Entry costs (stamp duty + legal)
− Exit costs (CGT + agent + legal)
− Holding costs (tax + management + vacancy)
± FX gain/loss on total invested capital

Most investors only calculate the first line. Professionals calculate all seven. 真實報酬 =
資本增值（當地貨幣）
+ 累計淨租金收入
− 進場成本（印花稅+律師費）
− 退場成本（資本利得稅+仲介+律師）
− 持有成本（稅+管理+空租）
± 總投入資本的匯率損益

多數投資人只算第一項。專業投資人算完全部七項。
