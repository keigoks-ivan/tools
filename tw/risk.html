Key Risk Factors主要風險因素

Credit Control Squeeze信用管制壓力

7th wave unprecedented — 2nd home no grace period, 3rd+ at 30% LTV. Pre-sale transactions collapsed -74.7%. Bank mortgage outstanding exceeds NT$13 trillion (35% of total lending). Any relaxation would be a major catalyst; continued tightening means prolonged market freeze.第七波史無前例 — 第二戶取消寬限期、第三戶以上貸款成數僅30%。預售屋成交量暴跌 -74.7%。銀行房貸餘額逾13兆元（佔總放款35%）。任何鬆綁將成重大催化劑；持續緊縮則市場長期凍結。

Delivery Tsunami交屋海嘯

40 wan+ units delivering 2025-2027. Net effective supply 3-5 wan/year after replacement demand. But unsold inventory at 11.1 wan (record high). Three-phase risk: transfer wave → cancellation wave → foreclosure wave (tail risk). Regional oversupply pockets in Taoyuan, Tainan most vulnerable.2025-2027年交屋量逾40萬戶。扣除替換需求後淨有效供給每年3-5萬戶。但餘屋量達11.1萬戶（歷史新高）。三階段風險：交屋潮 → 退戶潮 → 法拍潮（尾端風險）。桃園、台南區域性供過於求最脆弱。

Demographic Collapse人口崩塌

Fertility rate 0.72 — contender for world's lowest. Population declining 59 consecutive months. 2025 births just 107,800 (-20% YoY). BUT mitigating factors: households peak 2041, foreign workers 83.3 wan rising to 100 wan+ by 2028, single-person households now 36% of total and growing.出生率0.72 — 角逐全球最低。人口連續59個月下降。2025年出生僅107,800人（年減20%）。但有緩和因素：家戶數2041年才見頂、外勞83.3萬人將於2028年突破100萬、單人家戶已佔36%且持續增加。

Geopolitical地緣政治

Taiwan Strait tensions remain a persistent overhang. Foreign institutional investors increasingly factor cross-strait risk into exposure limits. While markets have largely priced in baseline risk, any escalation could trigger capital flight and sharp repricing of property assets.台海緊張局勢持續成為市場隱憂。外資機構投資者日益將兩岸風險納入曝險上限考量。雖然市場已大致反映基線風險，任何升級都可能引發資金外逃和房產資產劇烈重新定價。
