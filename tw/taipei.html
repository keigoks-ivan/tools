MACRO & POLICY FRAMEWORK總體經濟與調控政策框架
Taiwan's 7th wave of selective credit controls (September 2024) was the most aggressive in history — cutting LTV on 3rd+ properties and high-value homes to 30%, and reducing 2nd-home LTV nationwide to 50% (later eased to 60% for owner-occupiers). Taipei is the hardest-hit city: average LTV fell from 63% (Q1 2022 peak) to 58% in Q3 2024 — a 5-point drop vs 3-point nationally. Combined with housing tax 2.0 (Houchang Tax), purchase costs have risen at three levels simultaneously. AI and semiconductor-driven stock market wealth (TAIEX above 30,000) provides a crucial buffer: high-net-worth buyers hold sufficient cash to bypass the LTV constraints. 台灣2024年9月實施第七波選擇性信用管制——史上最嚴——第三戶以上、豪宅貸款成數上限壓至三成，全國第二戶統一降至五成（後調整換屋至六成）。台北市是受創最深的城市：平均房貸成數從2022年Q1高點的63%驟降至2024年Q3的58%，跌幅達5個百分點，全國平均僅跌3%。加上囤房稅2.0，持有成本出現三重加重。然而AI與半導體產業強勢推動台股突破三萬點，財富效應為市場提供重要緩衝：高資產買方持有充沛自有資金，不受貸款成數制約。

SUPPLY: PRESALE CONTRACTION & NEW INVENTORY WAVE供給面：預售市場收縮與新屋供給浪潮
Presale filings collapsed to just 994 units in Q4 2025 — the lowest quarterly count since 2019. Developers are delaying launches and downsizing projects as credit controls bite. However, national unsold new-home inventory has swelled to 111,000+ units, the highest since tracking began. The 2025–2027 delivery wave (400,000 units nationally) will test buyer commitment: failed mortgage approvals at settlement could trigger a secondary-market listing surge. 2025年Q4預售申報量驟降至僅994戶——2019年以來單季最低。信用管制衝擊下，開發商延後推案並縮小規模。然而，全國待售新成屋庫存膨脹至11萬+戶，創統計以來新高。2025-2027年交屋潮（全國約40萬戶）將考驗買方承諾：交屋時房貸核拒可能引發中古市場拋售潮。

DEMOGRAPHICS: PAPER MIGRATION & MICRO-HOUSEHOLDS人口結構：帳面遷徙與一人家戶崛起
Taipei's net migration has been negative every month in 2025, losing over 33,000 registered residents in H1 alone. This is largely "paper migration" — households re-registering in New Taipei or Taoyuan for tax and school-district reasons. Crucially, single-person households now exceed 42% in Taipei, permanently reshaping housing demand toward compact formats. The 1+1BR and studio segments have doubled their market share since 2022. 台北市2025年每月淨遷出皆為負值，上半年累計流失逾33,000名設籍人口。這主要是「帳面遷徙」——家戶為了稅務或學區因素將戶籍遷往新北或桃園。關鍵趨勢：台北一人家戶佔比已突破42%，永久改變住宅需求結構，轉向小坪數產品。1+1房與套房自2022年以來市佔率翻倍。

TRANSACTION MARKET: VOLUME COLLAPSE & BUYER'S MARKET買賣市場：量縮探底與買方定價時代
Taipei building transfers fell to ~24,000 in 2025, with Jan–Feb 2026 annualizing at just 22,050 — the lowest since 2016. Negotiation power has shifted decisively to buyers: 60% of deals now involve >10% price cuts from listing, vs 30% a year ago. The housing price index has declined for 3 consecutive quarters, yet presale prices paradoxically rose +4.81% QoQ as developers hold firm on new launches. This price divergence between new and secondary markets is the defining feature of the current cycle. 台北市2025年建物移轉棟數降至約24,000棟，2026年1-2月年化僅22,050棟——2016年以來最低。議價權已決定性地轉向買方：60%成交案議價幅度超過10%，一年前僅30%。住宅價格指數連續三季下滑，但預售價格反而季增+4.81%——開發商堅守新案定價。新舊市場的價格背離是本輪週期的核心特徵。

DISTRICT PRICE MAP — 2026 Q1各行政區房價地圖——2026年Q1
Taipei's 12 districts show a clear three-tier structure. The premium core (Xinyi/Da'an) trades at 120–250 NT$/ping, driven by luxury and prestige demand. The tech corridor (Neihu/Nangang) at 80–120 commands a premium from proximity to the Neihu Tech Park and the emerging Nangang biotech hub. Peripheral districts (Wenshan/Shilin/Beitou) at 45–65 offer the only entry points for first-time buyers and are where social housing is concentrated. 台北12區呈現清晰的三級結構。核心精華區（信義/大安）每坪120-250萬，由豪宅與品牌需求驅動。科技走廊（內湖/南港）80-120萬/坪，受惠於內科與南港生技園區。外圍區（文山/士林/北投）45-65萬/坪，是首購族唯一入場機會，也是社會住宅集中區域。

COMMERCIAL: OFFICE MARKET & BTR商用不動產：辦公室市場與租屋新制
Taipei's office market faces a structural inflection in 2026. Nearly 59,000 ping of Grade A supply will enter the market (CBD + Nangang), pushing vacancy from ~3% toward 10%+. This is the first genuine tenant's market in a decade. ESG-certified and green buildings will absorb demand; legacy buildings face downgrade pressure. The Dunbei corridor led rent growth at +7.59% YoY, while overall CBD rents rose only +1.7%. 台北辦公室市場2026年面臨結構性轉折。近5.9萬坪A辦新供給將入市（CBD+南港），空置率從約3%推向10%以上。這是十年來首次真正的租戶市場。ESG認證與綠建築將吸收需求；傳統舊大樓面臨降級壓力。敦北走廊租金年增+7.59%領漲，但CBD整體租金僅增+1.7%。

Deleveraging Deep Water去槓桿化深水區

Delivery Wave — Biggest Downside Risk交屋潮賣壓——最大下行風險

400,000 pre-sale deliveries nationally in 2025–2027. Failed loans at delivery → forced secondary market listings. Combined with 111K+ unsold national inventory, creates structural price ceiling for next 2-3 years.全台2025-2027年約40萬戶預售屋交屋潮。交屋時貸款核拒→被迫中古市場拋售。加上全國11萬+待售新成屋，形成未來2-3年的結構性價格天花板。

Rental Sector — Strongest Segment租賃逆勢升溫——最強板塊

Micro-Housing Irreversible小宅化不可逆——產品革命
