Taichung has emerged as central Taiwan's property hotspot, driven by MRT Green Line completion, multiple redevelopment zones (14th, 7th rezone areas), and population inflow from Taipei. The city's median multiple of 11.9x remains high but offers better value than Taipei's 15.41x. The redevelopment zones in Beitun and Xitun districts have seen the fastest appreciation.

台中已成為中台灣房市最熱門的城市，受益於捷運綠線通車、多個重劃區（14期、7期）開發，以及台北移入人口。房價所得比11.9倍仍偏高，但相對台北的15.41倍更具可負擔性。北屯和西屯的重劃區漲幅最為可觀。
