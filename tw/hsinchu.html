MARKET OVERVIEW市場概覽

Hsinchu's property market is primarily driven by TSMC and the broader semiconductor industry. The Hsinchu Science Park (HSP) employs over 160,000 workers, creating sustained housing demand that has pushed prices up 58.4% over five years — the highest appreciation rate of any major city in Taiwan.新竹房地產市場主要受台積電及半導體產業驅動。新竹科學園區（竹科）僱用超過16萬名員工，持續創造住房需求，推動房價五年內上漲58.4% — 為全台主要城市中最高漲幅。

The Zhubei redevelopment area has emerged as the hottest corridor, with new high-rise developments catering to HSP families seeking modern amenities and school districts. Spillover demand has extended south to Toufen and Zhunan in Miaoli County, where prices remain 30-40% below Zhubei levels.竹北重劃區已成為最熱門的走廊，新建高層社區滿足竹科家庭對現代設施和學區的需求。外溢需求已向南延伸至苗栗縣的頭份和竹南，當地價格仍低於竹北30-40%。

Key Risk: Concentration in a single industry. Any significant downturn in the global semiconductor cycle could impact employment and housing demand simultaneously.主要風險：單一產業集中度高。全球半導體周期若出現重大下行，可能同時衝擊就業及住房需求。

KEY INSIGHTS關鍵洞察

TSMC Drive台積電驅動

Semiconductor investment sustains demand. TSMC's continued capex expansion in Hsinchu anchors long-term housing fundamentals.半導體投資持續支撐需求。台積電在新竹持續擴大資本支出，為長期住房基本面提供穩固支撐。

Zhubei Premium竹北溢價

Redevelopment area commands a significant premium over older districts, driven by new supply quality and lifestyle positioning.重劃區相對舊市區享有顯著溢價，受新建案品質及生活定位驅動。

Yield Compression收益率壓縮

Low yields at 1.9% due to rapid price appreciation outpacing rental growth. Negative carry after mortgage costs.因房價漲幅遠超租金成長，收益率僅1.9%。扣除房貸成本後為負現金流。

Concentration Risk集中度風險

Single-industry dependency on semiconductors. A cyclical downturn could trigger simultaneous job losses and price declines.對半導體產業的單一依賴。景氣循環下行可能同步引發失業及房價下跌。
