Valuation Framework估值框架

Managed Bubble管理型泡沫

All valuation indicators are at historic extremes, but four structural pillars prevent collapse: high down payment requirements (30-50% LTV for non-first homes), long-term fully-amortizing mortgages, continuous policy fine-tuning by CBC, and deeply ingrained cultural psychology favoring homeownership over renting.所有估值指標均處於歷史極端，但四大結構性支柱防止崩盤：高自備款要求（非首購 30-50% LTV）、長期完全攤還房貸、央行持續微調政策、以及根深蒂固的「有土斯有財」文化心理。

Rental Catch-Up租金追趕

CPI rent surged +2.66% YoY, a 28.5-year high. Rents are accelerating toward prices, which may gradually improve price-to-rent ratios from their extreme levels. This narrowing dynamic suggests the market is self-correcting via rental inflation rather than price declines.CPI 租金年增 +2.66%，創 28.5 年新高。租金正加速追趕房價，可能逐步改善極端的租金比。這種收斂動態表明市場正透過租金通膨而非房價下跌進行自我修正。

Construction Cost Floor營建成本底部

Labor and materials form a hard price floor at 25-27 wan/ping construction cost. Developers prefer to delay launches rather than sell below cost. This cost floor effectively limits downside risk in new construction pricing, creating a structural support level for the broader market.勞動力與建材形成每坪 25-27 萬的硬性價格底部。建商寧可延後推案也不願賠本出售。此營建成本底線有效限制了新建案價格下行風險，為整體市場提供結構性支撐。
