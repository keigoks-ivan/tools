1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY1. 執行摘要

Taoyuan is Taiwan's fastest-growing city by population (+21,203 net migration/year, highest nationally) and the most affordable among six cities (PIR 8.65x, lowest). Average age ~37.5 years (youngest). But it faces the highest new construction supply pressure — old housing share is only 40.7% (lowest, meaning most supply is pure new build, not replacement). Unsold inventory: 1.9万 units. 2025 transactions -20% YoY.

The Qingpu HSR special zone is the market's bellwether: pre-sale volume crashed -93.4% at A18, new case sales progress only 21%. Short-term oversupply is severe, but long-term fundamentals (population growth, HSR 19min to Taipei, Airport MRT, Cathay NT$26B station-front project) remain compelling.

桃園是台灣人口增長最快的城市（年淨遷入+21,203人，全國最高）且六都中最可負擔（PIR 8.65倍，最低）。平均年齡約37.5歲（最年輕）。但面臨最高的新建供給壓力——老屋比例僅40.7%（最低，意味大部分供給是純新建而非替換）。餘屋1.9萬戶。2025年成交-20%。

青埔高鐵特區是市場風向球：A18預售量暴跌-93.4%，新案銷售進度僅21%。短期供過於求嚴重，但長期基本面（人口增長、高鐵19分鐘到台北、機場捷運、國泰260億站前計畫）仍具說服力。

2. POPULATION MAGNET2. 人口磁鐵效應

Taoyuan's 7-year cumulative net migration: +126,000 people. Population 2,355,512 (Feb 2026). The primary driver: Taipei price refugees seeking HSR-commutable affordable housing. Taoyuan PIR 8.65x vs Taipei 16.43x — nearly half the burden. Average age 37.5 (youngest among six cities, elderly share only 14–15%). Population projected to continue growing through 2035+.

This demographic advantage is Taoyuan's single most important structural support: a growing, young, working-age population creates persistent baseline demand that no other Taiwanese city can match outside of Hsinchu's tech corridor.

桃園7年累計淨遷入：+126,000人。人口2,355,512（2026年2月）。主要驅動力：台北房價難民尋找高鐵通勤可負擔住宅。桃園PIR 8.65倍 vs 台北16.43倍——負擔幾乎減半。平均年齡37.5歲（六都最年輕，老年比僅14–15%）。人口預計持續增長至2035年+。

這項人口優勢是桃園最重要的結構性支撐：持續增長的年輕工作人口創造基本面需求，在新竹科技走廊以外，台灣沒有其他城市能匹及。

4. QINGPU HSR SPECIAL ZONE4. 青埔高鐵特區

Qingpu HSR Special Zone (490 hectares, A17/A18/A19 MRT stations) is Taiwan's most closely watched new development area. After a 70–80% price surge in 5 years (2021 avg 30–38万 → 2025 avg 51–54万/ping), the market has entered correction phase.

青埔高鐵特區（490公頃，A17/A18/A19捷運站）是台灣最受關注的新開發區。經歷5年70–80%漲幅（2021年均價30–38萬 → 2025年均價51–54萬/坪），市場已進入修正階段。

5. SUPPLY-DEMAND BALANCE5. 供需平衡

Taoyuan's supply-demand imbalance is the most severe among six cities. The combination of highest new construction ratio (lowest old housing share) and frozen transaction volume creates a prolonged digestion cycle.

桃園的供需失衡是六都中最嚴重的。最高的新建比例（最低老屋佔比）加上凍結的交易量，造成漫長的去化週期。

8. OUTLOOK8. 展望

Short-term (2026–2027): Qingpu oversupply persists for 1–2 years. Price correction 5–15% depending on scenario. Base case 55% probability. Builder pullback of -30%+ in new launches partially self-corrects supply, but 3,336 unit delivery wave must be absorbed first.

Long-term (2028–2032): Population growth + Cathay Station Front Plaza (Phase 1: 2030–31) + MRT Green Line (2028) = recovery to new highs by 2030–2032. Taoyuan as a whole: short-term pressure from supply overhang, long-term positive from population and affordability advantage. The city's structural edge — youngest demographics, lowest PIR, HSR connectivity — ensures it will outperform peripheral cities in the recovery cycle.

短期（2026–2027）：青埔供過於求持續1–2年。價格修正5–15%視情境而定。基本情境55%機率。建商推案縮減-30%+部分自我修正供給，但3,336戶交屋潮須先消化。

長期（2028–2032）：人口增長 + 國泰站前廣場（第一期：2030–31）+ 捷運綠線（2028）= 2030–2032年回復新高。桃園整體：短期承受供給壓力，長期受惠人口與可負擔優勢。這座城市的結構性優勢——最年輕人口、最低PIR、高鐵聯通性——確保它在復甦週期中將跑贏外圍城市。
