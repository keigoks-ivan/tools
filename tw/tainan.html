THE TSMC EFFECT台積電效應
STSP houses TSMC's advanced packaging operations (CoWoS), the most critical bottleneck in the global AI supply chain. STSP revenue has surpassed Hsinchu Science Park, making Tainan the de facto center of Taiwan's semiconductor industry. Engineering salary premiums run 30–50% above the Tainan average, creating a "dual economy": TSMC workers can afford NT$40–50万/ping while local workers are stuck at NT$20–25万/ping. Shanhua, Shanshang, and Xinshi districts around STSP saw 80–100%+ price increases in just 3 years. 南科是台積電先進封裝（CoWoS）的生產基地——全球AI供應鏈最關鍵的瓶頸。南科營收已超越竹科，使台南實質成為台灣半導體產業中心。工程師薪資溢價達台南平均水準的30-50%，形成「雙元經濟」：台積電員工可負擔NT$40-50萬/坪，當地工人卻只能承擔NT$20-25萬/坪。園區周邊的善化、山上、新市區域在3年內房價飆漲80-100%以上。

SUPPLY-DEMAND DYNAMICS供需動態
Unsold inventory at 1.1万 units is manageable — second lowest among six cities. Pre-sale volume collapsed -80%+ YoY as developers pulled back aggressively. However, STSP employment creates steady absorption for well-located projects near the science park. The key risk lies in non-STSP peripheral areas with no industrial support, where absorption rates are collapsing. The market has bifurcated into "STSP-supported" and "everything else." 待售庫存1.1萬戶尚屬可控——六都第二低。預售量年減逾-80%，開發商大幅縮手。然而，南科就業持續為園區附近優質建案創造穩定去化。關鍵風險在於無產業支撐的非南科外圍區域，去化率正在崩跌。市場已分裂為「南科支撐區」和「其他區域」。
PRICE TREND: BENEFIT CYCLE ENDING價格趨勢：紅利週期尾聲
Tainan experienced a 5-year price surge driven entirely by TSMC expansion. Chiayi Taibao (STSP-adjacent) saw +113% appreciation. The market is now correcting: 2025 average price fell -4.32%. Tainan is entering its "benefit cycle end" phase. Prices are unlikely to return to pre-TSMC levels, but a 5–10% correction from peak is expected before stabilization. RENTAL MARKET租賃市場
Tainan yields range from ~2.8–3.5% — meaningfully better than Taipei. STSP employee rental demand is strong and relatively inelastic. 2BR near STSP rents at NT$15,000–22,000/month. Dormitory and company housing programs also absorb a portion of demand, somewhat capping market rents near the science park. The rental market is healthiest in STSP-adjacent areas and weakest in traditional Tainan districts where population continues to age. 台南收益率約2.8-3.5%——明顯優於台北。南科員工租賃需求強勁且相對缺乏彈性。南科附近兩房月租NT$15,000-22,000。宿舍及企業住房也吸收部分需求，在一定程度上限制了園區附近的市場租金。租賃市場在南科鄰近區域最健康，在人口持續老化的傳統台南區域最為疲弱。

The TSMC-driven benefit cycle is ending. Pre-sale volume has collapsed. Credit controls restrict investor activity. Expect 5–8% price correction across Tainan, with steeper declines in non-STSP peripheral zones and more resilience near the science park.台積電驅動的紅利週期正在結束。預售量崩跌。信用管制限制投資客活動。預期台南整體修正5-8%，非南科外圍區域跌幅更大，園區附近較具韌性。

STSP Expansion: Long-Term Positive南科擴張：長期正面

Advanced packaging demand (CoWoS) remains insatiable globally. STSP expansion continues with new fab capacity. Long-term fundamentals are positive for STSP-adjacent areas. Engineer migration provides a structural demand floor that non-STSP Tainan lacks.全球先進封裝（CoWoS）需求仍供不應求。南科持續擴充產能。南科鄰近區域長期基本面正向。工程師移入提供結構性需求下檔，這是非南科台南所缺乏的。

Non-STSP Tainan: Slower Recovery非南科台南：復甦較慢

Traditional Tainan districts face population outflow in rural areas, aging demographics, and no industrial catalyst. Recovery will lag STSP zones by 1–2 years. Old housing stock (57.9% over 30yr) limits quality supply but also caps buyer enthusiasm.傳統台南區域面臨鄉村人口外流、人口老化、缺乏產業催化劑。復甦將落後南科區域1-2年。老屋存量高（57.9%逾30年）限制優質供給，也抑制買氣。

Watch: TSMC Global Allocation Shifts觀察：台積電全球產能配置變化

TSMC's Arizona and Kumamoto fabs represent production diversification. Any material shift in advanced packaging allocation away from STSP would fundamentally alter Tainan's demand equation. Monitor TSMC capex announcements and STSP hiring trends as leading indicators.台積電亞利桑那和熊本廠代表產能分散化。若先進封裝產能配置顯著移出南科，將根本改變台南的需求方程式。追蹤台積電資本支出公告和南科徵才趨勢作為領先指標。
