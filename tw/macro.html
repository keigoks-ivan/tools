Monetary Cycle × House Prices貨幣週期 × 房價
What to watch: Taiwan's HPI barely responded to GFC (2008–09) — domestic demand is resilient. The real inflection came from the 2020 COVID rate cut plus M2 surge to 8.7%, launching a multi-year price surge that outlasted the rate hike cycle.
重點觀察：台灣HPI對GFC幾乎無感，顯示內需韌性極強。真正的轉折點來自2020年COVID降息加上M2暴增至8.7%，啟動了超越升息週期的多年房價上漲。

Real Economy × House Prices實體經濟 × 房價
What to watch: Building permits (dashed) turned sharply negative in 2015–16 after the 房地合一稅, constraining new supply — explaining why prices barely fell despite cooling demand. Today, permits are contracting again (−19% in 2023), setting up another supply squeeze.
重點觀察：建照核發量（虛線）在房地合一稅後2015–16年急劇轉負，壓縮新供給——這解釋了為何即使需求降溫，房價跌幅有限。目前建照再度收縮（2023年−19%），醞釀另一波供給緊縮。

Key Cycle Events (2008–2025)關鍵週期事件（2008–2025）
2008–2009 · GFC SHOCK2008–2009 · GFC衝擊
CBC Cuts to 1.25% — HPI HoldsCBC降息至1.25%，HPI撐住
Global crisis triggers CBC rate cuts. GDP falls −1.6% in 2009. Unlike most markets, Taiwan's HPI only dips −3% — domestic end-user demand provides a floor. Recovery begins swiftly in 2010.全球危機觸發CBC降息，2009年GDP衰退1.6%。不同於多數市場，台灣HPI僅跌3%——自用需求提供了支撐，2010年迅速展開反彈。
2010–2015 · POST-GFC BOOM2010–2015 · 後GFC榮景
CBC Hikes, HPI Surges +46%CBC升息，HPI累漲46%
Low global rates fuel speculative buying. CBC hikes to 1.875% (2011) to curb speculation. Luxury Tax (奢侈稅) introduced Jun 2011 — short-term resale suppressed, not prices. HPI rises from 68 to near 100.全球低利率帶動投機買盤。CBC升息至1.875%以抑制投機，2011年6月實施奢侈稅——壓制短期轉手，並非房價本身。HPI從68升至近100。
JUL 2016 · TAX REFORM2016年7月 · 稅制改革
房地合一稅 Cools Transaction Volume房地合一稅冷卻成交量
Combined House & Land Tax replaces the Luxury Tax. Capital gains taxed up to 45% for short-term sellers. Volume collapses to historic lows. HPI plateaus near 100 for 4 years — quantity adjusts, not price.房地合一稅取代奢侈稅，短期賣方資本利得稅率高達45%。成交量跌至歷史低位，HPI在100附近盤整近4年——調整的是量，不是價。
MAR 2020 · COVID STIMULUS2020年3月 · COVID刺激
CBC Cuts to 1.125%, M2 Surges 8.7%CBC降至1.125%，M2激增8.7%
Taiwan's COVID impact is mild (GDP +3.1%) but rate cut floods market with liquidity. Repatriated overseas Taiwanese capital seeks property. HPI accelerates +7.8% in 2020, +8.5% in 2021 — the fastest pace since 2013.台灣COVID衝擊有限（GDP+3.1%），但降息釋放大量流動性。回台台商資金湧入房市，HPI 2020年漲7.8%、2021年漲8.5%，為2013年以來最快升速。
2021 · SUPPLY SURGE2021年 · 供給衝高
Building Permits +13.8%, HPI +8.5%建照核發+13.8%，HPI+8.5%
Developers rush to meet demand. New building permits surge to decade highs. Despite supply response, prices keep rising — demand from domestic buyers, overseas Taiwanese, and TSMC-region workers absorbs all new units.開發商搶供應需求，建照核發衝至十年高點。儘管供給回應，房價仍持續上漲——內需、回台台商及台積電園區員工消化所有新建單位。
MAR 2022 · RATE HIKE CYCLE2022年3月 · 升息週期
CBC First Hike in 11 Years + Credit ControlsCBC 11年來首次升息 + 選擇性信用管制
CBC raises OPR four times in 2022 (1.125%→1.75%). Simultaneous 6th round of Selective Credit Controls restricts bank LTV for second homes. HPI peaks at 151 in 2023 — 4 hikes fail to break the bull trend.CBC 2022年升息4次（1.125%→1.75%）。同期第六次選擇性信用管制限制第二戶LTV。HPI於2023年見頂151——4次升息仍未打破多頭趨勢。
2023–2024 · PRICE PEAK & RETREAT2023–2024 · 房價見頂回落
HPI Peaks 151 → Softens to 149HPI見頂151後回落至149
Transaction volume collapses to historic low. Build permits −19% in 2023. HPI slips −1.3% in 2024 — first annual decline since 2016. CBC holds rate at 2.00%. Quantity continues adjusting, price holds near highs.成交量跌至歷史低位，建照2023年減少19%。HPI 2024年微跌1.3%，為2016年以來首次年度下跌。CBC利率維持2.00%，量縮價穩局面持續。
2025 · AI & SEMICONDUCTOR CATALYST2025年 · AI與半導體催化
GDP +8.68% Driven by AI ExportsAI出口驅動GDP+8.68%
Taiwan's GDP surges on AI chip demand — TSMC Hsinchu/Taichung fabs at full capacity. Hsinchu Science Park area sees fierce demand from TSMC engineers. HPI at 145 (Q3 2025), Hsinchu area significantly outperforms.AI晶片需求帶動台灣GDP爆發，台積電竹科/台中廠產能滿載。竹科周邊因台積電工程師搶房需求旺盛，HPI達145（2025 Q3），竹科區域顯著跑贏大盤。
