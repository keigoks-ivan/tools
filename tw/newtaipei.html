EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要
New Taipei is Taiwan's most populous city (4M+ residents). Average home price ~NT$12M. Unsold inventory stands at 2.0万 units — the highest among all six cities. 2025 transactions fell -22% YoY. Price-to-income ratio: 14.03x. Old housing stock: 51.1% over 30 years (94.8万 units — the absolute highest nationally). The market is deeply bifurcated: egg-yolk districts (Banqiao, Zhonghe, Yonghe) remain stable, while peripheral redevelopment zones (Wugu Zhouzaiyang, Yingge Fengming) face severe oversupply pressure. Population shows slight net inflow. 新北市是台灣人口最多的城市（400萬+居民）。平均房價約NT$1,200萬。待售庫存達2.0萬戶——六都最高。2025年交易量年減-22%。房價所得比14.03倍。老屋比例：51.1%屋齡逾30年（94.8萬戶，全國絕對值最高）。市場呈現明顯兩極化：蛋黃區（板橋、中和、永和）穩定；外圍重劃區（五股洲子洋、鶯歌鳳鳴）面臨嚴重供給過剩壓力。人口呈現微幅淨流入。

MARKET STRUCTURE: 29 DISTRICTS, 4-5 SUB-MARKETS市場結構：29區、4-5個次市場
New Taipei's 29 districts show extreme variation. Banqiao functions as a mini-Taipei (avg 55–65万/ping). Yonghe/Zhonghe are mature communities (45–55万/ping). Tucheng/Xindian represent mid-range options (35–45万/ping). Wugu/Linkou/Sanxia offer affordability but carry high supply risk (25–35万/ping). Key insight: New Taipei is NOT one market — it is 4–5 distinct sub-markets with fundamentally different risk-return profiles. 新北市29個行政區呈現極端分化。板橋功能上等同「小台北」（均價55-65萬/坪）。永和/中和為成熟社區（45-55萬/坪）。土城/新店為中階選擇（35-45萬/坪）。五股/林口/三峽提供可負擔性但供給風險高（25-35萬/坪）。關鍵洞察：新北不是一個市場——而是4-5個風險報酬特性完全不同的次市場。

SUPPLY PRESSURE供給壓力
New Taipei holds 2.0万 unsold units — the highest of all six cities. 2024 completion area reached 243万m². Wugu Zhouzaiyang and Yingge Fengming are the two most oversupplied redevelopment zones in all of Taiwan. An expected 10%+ price correction in peripheral zones is already materializing. Developer concessions (free interior fit-out, parking upgrades, extended payment terms) are appearing with increasing frequency. 新北市待售庫存達2.0萬戶——六都最高。2024年完工面積達243萬平方公尺。五股洲子洋和鶯歌鳳鳴是全台供給最過剩的兩個重劃區。外圍區域預期10%以上的價格修正已在發生。開發商讓利措施（免費裝潢、車位升級、延長付款期）出現頻率日益增加。

DEMAND DRIVERS需求驅動因素
Primary demand driver: Taipei spillover — New Taipei is ~50% cheaper than Taipei core, making it the natural destination for priced-out buyers. MRT connectivity continues to improve (Circular Line, Sanying Line). The New Youth Loan program supports first-time buyers. However, the 7th wave credit controls suppress upgraders and investors, limiting transaction velocity. The result is a market sustained almost entirely by owner-occupier first-time buyer demand. 主要需求驅動力：台北外溢效應——新北市價格約為台北核心區的50%，是被排擠買方的自然選擇。捷運持續擴展（環狀線、三鶯線）。新青安貸款支持首購族。然而，第七波信用管制壓抑換屋族和投資客，限制交易量。結果是市場幾乎完全依賴自住首購需求支撐。
RENTAL & YIELD租賃與收益率
Gross yield ranges from ~2.0–2.8% across New Taipei. Monthly rent for 2BR: NT$18,000–28,000 (Banqiao to Yonghe corridor). Vacancy is rising in new completions, particularly in peripheral redevelopment zones where rental demand is thin. CPI rent component increased +2.66% YoY, gradually catching up to price appreciation but from a very low base. 新北市毛收益率約2.0-2.8%。兩房月租：NT$18,000-28,000（板橋至永和走廊）。新完工物件空置率上升，尤其是租賃需求薄弱的外圍重劃區。CPI租金分項年增+2.66%，逐步追趕房價漲幅，但基期偏低。

OUTLOOK展望
Egg-Yolk Districts: Stable蛋黃區：穩定

Banqiao, Zhonghe, Yonghe expected to hold within -5% to flat. Limited new supply, strong transport connectivity, and Taipei spillover demand provide a floor. These districts function as independent micro-markets insulated from peripheral oversupply.板橋、中和、永和預期維持在-5%至持平。新供給有限、交通便利、台北外溢需求提供價格下檔支撐。這些區域如同獨立的微型市場，不受外圍供給過剩影響。

Peripheral Zones: -10% to -15% Correction外圍重劃區：修正-10%至-15%

Wugu Zhouzaiyang, Yingge Fengming, and similar redevelopment zones face the steepest correction risk. Oversupply + thin rental demand + credit controls = inevitable price adjustment. Developer concessions signal the correction is underway.五股洲子洋、鶯歌鳳鳴等重劃區面臨最大修正風險。供給過剩+租賃需求薄弱+信用管制=不可避免的價格調整。開發商讓利訊號顯示修正已在進行。

2027–2028: Supply Peak Passes2027-2028年：供給高峰過後

The current delivery wave peaks in 2026–2027. As the supply pipeline thins, gradual stabilization is expected. Markets that survive the current oversupply without forced selling will recover first.目前交屋潮高峰在2026-2027年。隨著供給管線減少，預期逐步穩定。能在當前供給過剩中不出現拋售的市場將率先復甦。

Long-Term: Aging Stock = Replacement Demand長期：老屋 = 替代需求

With 94.8万 units aged 30+ years (national highest), New Taipei has massive long-term replacement demand. Urban renewal and reconstruction programs will create sustained development activity for decades, supporting the construction and real estate sectors structurally.擁有94.8萬戶30年以上老屋（全國最多），新北市有龐大的長期替代需求。都更和危老重建計畫將在未來數十年創造持續開發活動，對營建與不動產形成結構性支撐。
