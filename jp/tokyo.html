SUMMARY總結

Center 5 Premium都心5區溢價

The Center 5 wards command a 3-5x premium over outer wards. Global institutional capital continues to flow into Minato, Chiyoda, and Shibuya trophy assets.都心5區對外圍區溢價達3至5倍。全球機構資本持續流入港區、千代田及渋谷頂級資產。

Yamanote Value山手線價值帶

Setagaya and Meguro offer strong residential fundamentals at 40-60% below center ward pricing. Family-oriented demand remains resilient.世田谷及目黑提供堅實住宅基本面，價格僅為都心區的40-60%。家庭導向需求持續穩健。

Outer Opportunity外圍區機會

Adachi and Edogawa wards offer the highest yields in the 23-ward area. Infrastructure upgrades are narrowing the convenience gap with inner wards.足立及江戸川區提供23區內最高收益率。基礎建設升級正在縮小與內區的便利性差距。

Foreign Capital外資動向

Weak yen and BOJ rate normalization have attracted record foreign investment. Cross-border capital accounts for 30%+ of central Tokyo transactions above ¥100M.日圓貶值及BOJ利率正常化吸引創紀錄外資。跨境資本佔中央東京1億円以上交易的30%以上。
