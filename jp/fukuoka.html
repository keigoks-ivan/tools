Fukuoka Growth Story福岡成長故事

Fukuoka is Japan's fastest-growing major city, powered by the Tenjin Big Bang redevelopment project that is transforming the city center with 50+ building replacements by 2026. A thriving startup ecosystem, tech company migration from Tokyo, and strong population inflow from younger demographics make Fukuoka the best growth-plus-yield story in Japan's property market.福岡是日本成長最快的主要城市，天神Big Bang再開發計畫正以50棟以上建築更新改造市中心至2026年。蓬勃的新創生態系、科技公司從東京遷入，加上年輕族群的強勁人口流入，使福岡成為日本房地產市場中最佳的增值加收益標的。

KEY INSIGHTS關鍵洞察

Tenjin Big Bang天神Big Bang

The Tenjin Big Bang project will replace 50+ buildings in the city core by 2026, adding 1.7x floor area. This is the largest urban redevelopment in regional Japan and is creating a new wave of premium supply.天神Big Bang計畫將於2026年前更新市中心50棟以上建築，樓地板面積增加1.7倍。這是日本地方都市最大規模的都市再開發，正創造新一波頂級供給。

Population Growth人口成長

Fukuoka is one of the few Japanese cities with net population inflow. Young workers and IT professionals are migrating from Tokyo, attracted by lower living costs and high quality of life.福岡是日本少數淨人口流入的城市之一。年輕勞工及科技專業人士因較低生活成本及高生活品質而從東京遷入。

Best Yield最佳收益

At 4.98% gross yield, Fukuoka offers the highest returns among Japan's major cities. Outer zones can reach 6%+ for well-located compact units near transit.以4.98%毛收益率計算，福岡提供日本主要城市中最高回報。外圍區域的交通便利小坪數單位可達6%以上。

Growth Momentum成長動能

13 consecutive years of price increases with +21.8% YoY in 2024 — the highest growth rate among all Japanese cities. The combination of yield and appreciation is unmatched nationally.連續13年價格上漲，2024年+21.8%年增率為全日本城市最高。收益率加增值的組合在全國無可匹敵。
