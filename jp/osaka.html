Osaka Market Outlook大阪市場展望

Osaka's property market is being reshaped by three mega-catalysts: Expo 2025 on Yumeshima island, the Umeda 2nd Phase redevelopment transforming the north gate area, and the planned IR/casino resort. Combined with relative affordability vs Tokyo, these drivers are attracting both domestic and foreign capital at an accelerating pace.大阪房地產市場正受三大催化劑重塑：夢洲2025世博會、梅田第二期再開發改造北門區域，以及規劃中的IR賭場度假村。加上相較東京的相對可負擔性，這些驅動力正以加速態勢吸引國內外資本。
