Yield Spread利差分析

At +3.09%, Tokyo's yield spread over the BOJ rate remains one of the most attractive in the APAC region. Even after the BOJ's historic rate normalization cycle, property yields comfortably cover borrowing costs, providing a genuine carry trade for leveraged investors.東京租金回報率與 BOJ 利率利差達 +3.09%，仍為亞太地區最具吸引力之一。即使 BOJ 歷史性升息週期後，房產收益仍能輕鬆覆蓋借貸成本，為槓桿投資者提供真正的套利機會。

Regional Value區域價值

Osaka (27x) and Fukuoka (26x) remain significantly cheaper than Tokyo (37x) on a price-to-rent basis. These secondary cities offer better yield profiles while benefiting from domestic migration, infrastructure investment, and tourism-driven demand growth.大阪（27 倍）和福岡（26 倍）的租金比遠低於東京（37 倍）。這些二線城市提供更好的收益率，同時受益於國內人口遷移、基礎設施投資和旅遊帶動的需求增長。

Bubble Risk泡沫風險

Tokyo condo prices have surged +64% in four years, driven by foreign capital inflows, weak yen dynamics, and ultra-low rates. While fundamentals differ from the 1990s bubble, the pace of appreciation warrants caution — particularly in prime central Tokyo where price-to-income ratios exceed 15x.東京公寓價格四年內飆升 64%，受外資流入、日圓疲弱及超低利率推動。雖然基本面與 1990 年代泡沫不同，但升值速度仍需警惕，特別是東京都心房價所得比已超過 15 倍。
